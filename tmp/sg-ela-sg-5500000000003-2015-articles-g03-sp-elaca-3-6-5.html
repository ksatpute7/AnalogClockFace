Day 5: Spelling Test

  • Preselect the individuals for the partner test; students should be grouped in a way that allows both students to be successful spelling their words and clearly reading the word to the individual taking the test. Consider language, reading ability, dialect, and any special needs when assigning partners.

  • Explain that they are taking the spelling test with a partner so that they can help each other become better spellers.

  • During testing, circulate to monitor and support students.

  • Choose four spelling words that follow four different spelling patterns to give to the entire class when all students have finished taking their tests.

  • This will help you assess who has learned the various spelling patterns and who still needs additional practice.
