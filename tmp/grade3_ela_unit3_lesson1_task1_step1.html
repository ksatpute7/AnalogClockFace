Introduction

In this unit, you will learn about the physical and behavioral adaptations of animals. You will also learn how these adaptations allow animals to live in a particular environment. In this assessment unit, you will demonstrate your ability to do the following.

  • Comprehend informational texts
  • Take notes from informational texts
  • Write a report on animal adaptations
  • Research and include textual and visual information in your writing
  • Reflect on your writing
