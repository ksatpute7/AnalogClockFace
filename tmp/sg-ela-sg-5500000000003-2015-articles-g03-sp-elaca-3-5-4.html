Day 4: Spelling Practice

  • If necessary, remind students how to practice spelling words using the “See, Say, Cover, Write, Check” method.

  • Review the words with students:

    • amount
    • annoy
    • appoint
    • avoid
    • bounce
    • broil
    • choice
    • employ
    • hour
    • poison
    • proud
    • prowl
    • shower
    • thousand
    • voyage

  • Check that students understand the instructions they have been given for the “See, Say, Cover, Write, Check” method of spelling practice.

  • Review the results of their spelling practice and ask students what they will do to prepare for the test.

  • Help students with the available technology to make an audio recording of themselves reading the words aloud.
