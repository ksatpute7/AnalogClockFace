Genre Form

Pretend that you and your partner work in the local public library and that you have been asked to label all the books with their correct genres.

Complete the Genre Form.

  • Read each description.
  • Choose the genre for each text.
  • Compare your answer with your partner’s. If you disagree, talk about why you chose the genre that you did. See if you and your partner can come to an agreement.
