King Lion Character Profile

The characters in fables are usually animals, and the animals display very specific character traits. You learn from the animals based on the traits they display. Today you will identify the character traits of King Lion.

Follow along as your teacher reads aloud "King Lion and the Beetle."

  • As you follow along, annotate places in the text that show King Lion’s character traits. Be prepared to explain your thinking.

Contribute your ideas as your teacher creates a Character Profile of King Lion chart.
