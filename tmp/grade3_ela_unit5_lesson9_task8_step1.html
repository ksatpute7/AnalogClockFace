Work Time
Vocabulary Review

Listen and respond as a group to the following questions. Try to use your vocabulary words in your responses.

  • Would a candle help you if you were groping in the dark?
  • Would a greedy person give away money to people in need? Would a greedy person want more than what he or she has?
  • If a person is arrested for theft, what is his or her crime?
  • If you seize something, do you ask politely for it? What do you do?
  • Grandmother Spider snatched the sun and took it away. Did she do this slowly and gently? Demonstrate how she did it.
  • Name a time that you rejoiced, and describe it to your group.
