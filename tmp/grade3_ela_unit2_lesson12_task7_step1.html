Book Club

You have seen how colors can be connected to mood in poetry. It is also possible to connect color to mood in stories.

Work with your group to find passages in your Book Club book that show emotion or mood. What colors connect to these passages? Discuss the color connections and why you made them.

Discuss your responses to the three questions you chose during your last Book Club meeting for the book you are reading.
