Just Like Anna

You learned that one way a writer helps you understand the story is by including experiences that people can relate to.

Watch and listen as your teacher models making a comparison between Anna’s experience and a personal experience.

  • Next, look in Birdsong for Anna and find a time when Anna felt or acted like you. Write a comparison of the experiences in your Notebook. Remember to expand the sentences to include details that will make the experience real for the reader.

When your writing is complete, share your comparison with a partner. Listen to any suggestions from your partner that might make the sentences even better.
