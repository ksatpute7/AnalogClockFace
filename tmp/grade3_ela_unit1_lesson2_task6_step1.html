Setting Reading Goals

Think back to last year when you were a second grader. What were the reading goals you set for yourself in second grade? Turn and Talk with a partner about your goals.

Use these goals to help you prepare for being a reader in third grade.

  • Read Possible Reading Goals and choose three of the goals from the list (they may include the goals you set at the end of last year).
  • Add any goal that you want to set that is not on the list.
  • Record your three goals on the Reading Goals form.
  • Share your goals with your teacher.
