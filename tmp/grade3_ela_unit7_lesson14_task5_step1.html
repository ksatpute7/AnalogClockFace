The Tale of Despereaux

Continue to read The Tale of Despereaux by Kate DiCamillo. You may read this independently or with a partner.

You should read Chapters 19–36 by Lesson 15.

As you read, think about the following questions and write your thinking in your Notebook.

In Chapter 26, the narrator says, “And hope is like love… a ridiculous, wonderful, powerful thing.”

  • What does the narrator mean?
  • How can hope be powerful?
  • How does this apply to Miggery Sow?
