Beavers, Bees, and Alligators

There are many characteristics (traits) that are common to all communities. These videos about beavers, bees, and alligators show some of the characteristics of different animal communities.

  • After watching the beaver video, write in your Notebook some things you noticed about these animals and their communities. Specifically, what were the animals doing? Did they behave like humans in any way?
  • Then watch the bee and alligator videos and write down a few more things you noticed about these animals and their communities. Again, think about what the animals were doing. How did they behave in their communities?
