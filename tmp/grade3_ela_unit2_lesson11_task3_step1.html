WORK TIME
Author’s Purpose Discussion

Discuss the following questions with a partner and then with the class.

  • Why do you think Judith Viorst wrote the poem?
  • Who do you think Sara Steinberg is? Why would the author “cancel” her? Is this a positive way to deal with people we do not like? What are other ways to handle problems with friends?
  • What do we learn about the narrator in the second stanza? How does she give us so much information in so few words?
  • Some of the lines in the poem are positive and would help people, and some are negative and even hurtful. Which are positive? How can you tell? Which are negative? How can you tell?

Now read the poem aloud with your classmates. Enjoy the rhythm, repetition, and humor of the poem.
