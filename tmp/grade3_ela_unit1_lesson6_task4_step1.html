Boxes for Katje

Listen and follow along as your teacher reads Boxes for Katje.

As you listen, see if you can answer the question: Why did Rosie’s community send boxes to Katje?

Discuss Boxes for Katje with your class. Here are some questions to think about.

  • What is the setting for this story?
  • What do we know about living in Olst, Holland, at this time?
  • How are the boxes from Rosie and her community helping Katje and the people in Olst?
