Narrative Features

A feature is a key part or element of a genre. Add this term to your Vocabulary form.

  • Review Boxes for Katje.
  • Then Turn and Talk with your partner about Boxes for Katje. You can also think about other narratives you have read recently.
  • What narrative features do you find? Write down your ideas.

Now, work with your class to create a Features of Narrative Writing chart. Be specific about the features. For example, if a classmate says that one narrative feature is “things that happen,” you could make a more specific suggestion: “events.”
