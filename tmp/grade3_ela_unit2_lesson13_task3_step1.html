Poem Response Group

Listen as your teacher reviews the Response Group Checklist. Then sit with your Response Group and share your poem.

  • Complete a checklist for each group member’s work. Write your notes on the checklist while you confer with your Response Group partners.

Here are some tips that will help as you consider one another’s work:

  • Read your poem aloud so you can hear how the poem sounds.

  • Get feedback from the group on word choice, rhyme, rhythm, and repetition.

  • Compare your poem with your group members’ poems. What did they do differently? Did you get a good sense of what they were feeling in their poems?
