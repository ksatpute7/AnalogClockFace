Purposeful Dialogue

Now you are going to learn another strategy for developing narratives. Writers use purposeful dialogue to develop events or show how characters respond to different situations.

What is dialogue? How do you know when you are reading dialogue?

Writers of strong narratives use purposeful dialogue to:

1) Set up the situation for the reader and give background information

2) Move the plot along

3) Reveal information about characters like their traits, how they feel and what they think, and how they respond to others
