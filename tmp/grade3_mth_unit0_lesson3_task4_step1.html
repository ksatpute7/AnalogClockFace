Write Missing Numbers

Open the number chart and use the drawing layer to write on it. Then you can take a snapshot of the chart and place in your Notebook.

  • Write the numbers that are missing from this number chart.

  • Explain how the numbers you wrote follow the patterns in the hundred chart.
  • Work with your partner. Compare your work with your partner’s work. Challenge your partner to explain the patterns clearly and completely.
  • What questions did you ask yourself before you worked on this problem?
  • How are the numbers after 100 like the numbers before 100?
  • Can you continue the pattern from the hundred chart?
  • What was one of the missing numbers you wrote? How did you know that was the number?
