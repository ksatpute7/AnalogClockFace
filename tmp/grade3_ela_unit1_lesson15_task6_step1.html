Sentence Deconstruction

Sometimes the meaning of an unfamiliar word can be found in the sentence the word is used in.

Read this sentence from What is a Community? from A to Z:

“Many communities are interconnected, or joined to one another, because people know what other people in faraway places are doing.”

  • What does interconnected mean?
  • How do you know?

Discuss your responses with the class.
