Introduction to Response Groups

You are going to participate in a Response Group.

A Response Group is a group of writers who get together to share their work, and to give and receive feedback so that their writing can become stronger.

Watch and listen as your teacher and classmates model how to conduct a Response Group.

  • Take notes on what you see and hear. You will share your observations after the demonstration.
