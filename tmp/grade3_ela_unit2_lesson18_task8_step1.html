The Five Ws in Kids Helping Kids

Work with your teacher to find the five Ws on the first page of Kids Helping Kids.

Then turn to the first paragraph on the first page of the text. Work with your teacher to fill in the Five Ws form with the rest of the class.

Try the next paragraph on your own.

After you finish, sit with your group and compare your form with your group members’ forms.

Are they the same or different?

One person from each group will share with the rest of the class what was alike or different in the forms in the group.
