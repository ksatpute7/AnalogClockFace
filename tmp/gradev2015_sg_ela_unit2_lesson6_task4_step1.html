Week 6, Day 4: Spelling Practice
  • Practice your spelling words using the “See, Say, Cover, Write, Check” method. Your teacher will review the steps in this method.
    • See the word.
    • Say the word while looking at it. Does the spelling accurately reflect the sounds of the letters? If not, what is different?
    • Cover the word with your hand or a piece of paper. Close your eyes and try to “see” the word in your head.
    • Write the word.
    • Check your spelling by uncovering the word and comparing it with your spelling. Check it letter by letter. If it’s wrong, start again. If you are correct, go to the next word.
  • Make a recording of yourself spelling the words out loud.
