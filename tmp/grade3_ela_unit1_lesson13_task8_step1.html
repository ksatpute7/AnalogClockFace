Your School Community Discussion

It is hard to write about something you do not know a lot about. One of the things writers do to learn about a subject is research. Research can be interviewing people or reading about the topic. You are going to interview a community member to get information about your school community.

Think about the following question.

  • Who are the members of your school community?

You will work with a partner to do research in the form of an interview with one member of the school community.
