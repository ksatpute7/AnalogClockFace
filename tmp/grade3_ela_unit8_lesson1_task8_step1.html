“My Bed Is a Boat”

In the poem “My Bed Is a Boat,” Robert Louis Stevenson compares his bed to a boat that takes him places while he sleeps.

Annotating poems is a very helpful way to understand their meaning. Annotation includes highlighting or underlining words and phrases that you are unclear about, or writing questions. You can also write the meaning of each group of four lines. This is called the Gist.
