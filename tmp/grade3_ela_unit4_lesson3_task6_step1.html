Temporal Words and Phrases

Writers use temporal words and phrases to show time passing.

It is important to guide your reader through how time passes in your narrative. Temporal words and phrases help you do that. They give the reader clues about time.

Look at the chart of temporal words and phrases in Gram’s Angel Food Cake.

Turn and Talk with your partner about which words and phrases are specific to Gram’s Angel Food Cake and which are general and could be used in your narratives. What other words and phrases do you know that show time passing?
