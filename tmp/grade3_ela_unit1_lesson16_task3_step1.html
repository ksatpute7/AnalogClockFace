Practice With Conjunctions

Now, work with a partner to combine the following pairs of sentences, using a different conjunction each time. Remember, you may change the order of the sentences to stress the most important sentence of the pair.

  • I am a strong student. I like to read a lot.
  • Sheila watches a movie. Sheila eats popcorn.
  • Juan gets the part of the King in the play. Juan will study hard.
  • School is over today. Brandie will come over to my house.

Share the sentences you create.

Remember that using conjunctions to make the meaning of what you write clearer to the reader is a great revision strategy.
