WORK TIME
Choosing a Memory

You know that Birdsong for Anna is a memoir—a story based on Betty Ochester’s life.

You are going to have the opportunity to write your own memoir.

Listen as your teacher shares ideas about how to get started with making a list of events for your memoir.

Now it is your turn.

  • Make a list in your Notebook of events in your life that you might write about in your memoir.

  • Review your list and choose one event you would most like to include in your memoir.

  • Once you have chosen an event to write about, describe your memory to your partner.
