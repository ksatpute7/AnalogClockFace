Now you will revise your work by writing a new introduction to your Cold Write narrative using one of the strategies that we have learned.

Choose to focus on either character or setting in your introduction. You can reread the text’s introduction with the focus you have chosen—Gram’s Angel Food Cake for character or Boxes for Katje for setting—if you need to see an example.
