Week 9, Day 2: Compound Words Game

What do these words have in common:

  • homework

  • lawnmower

  • earring

Listen and respond as your teacher discusses compound words.

Then follow these steps to play a game in a group of four students.

  • Choose a spelling word with your partner, but don’t let the other pair know what the word is.
  • Draw a part of the spelling word while your partner draws the other part.
  • Show your pictures to the other pair. Have them guess what the word is.
  • Switch roles.
