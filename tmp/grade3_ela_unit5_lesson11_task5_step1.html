Reflect on Your Storytelling

You have worked hard to become a great storyteller. Today you will have the chance to see how much you have improved.

You will begin by watching the first story you told on your device. Then you will watch the next story you told.

When you have viewed both videos, write a reflection about your performances in your Notebook.

  • What changed from the first time you told your story to the last time?
  • What did you do well?
  • What still needs work?
  • How will this storytelling experience help you as a writer in the future?
