Consonant Hunt!

Time to search for the consonant patterns wr, kn, gn, and mb. You can look in any reading you like. Go on a Consonant Hunt!

With your group, select a few different readings and search for the consonant patterns wr, kn, gn, and mb.

Make a list of all the words you find, along with the title of the reading where you found each of them, in your Notebook.

Share your group’s list with the class.
