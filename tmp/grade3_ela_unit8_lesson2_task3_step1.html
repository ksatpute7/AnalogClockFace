“The Land of Counterpane”

Sit with a partner and read the remaining three stanzas of “The Land of Counterpane” aloud, one stanza at a time.

  • Discuss each stanza with your partner.
  • Annotate each stanza by writing notes on what each stanza is about and underlining any words or phrases you are unsure about.

Then share your findings with the rest of the class.
