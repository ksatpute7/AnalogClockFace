Possessives

Listen as your teacher tells you about possessive nouns.

Then work with a partner to rewrite these phrases using a possessive.

  • the eggs of the hen
  • the scarves of the boys
  • the football of the boys
  • the puppies of Diana
  • the bicycle of Jamal
  • the train of the engineer
  • the honey of the bees
  • the makeup of the clowns
