The Tale of Despereaux

Continue to read The Tale of Despereaux. You may read independently or with a partner.

You should read Chapters 1–18 by Lesson 11.

As you read, think about the following question.

  • In Chapter 6, it reads: “‘Don’t you understand?’ shouted Lester. ‘He must be punished. He must be brought up before the tribunal.’” Why did Lester, Despereaux’s father, turn him in to the tribunal? Why would a parent do that to his child?
  • Write down your response to this question in your Notebook or the Main Idea Thinking Guide Form.
