Week 24, Day 3: Present Spelling Words

Work with your group to introduce two of your spelling words to the class. Here are the things you will need to share about your words:

  • The number of letters there are in your word.
  • The number of syllables there are in your word.
  • The correct pronunciation of your word.
  • The word’s part of speech (noun, verb, adjective, or adverb).
  • The word’s definition.
  • Some of the word’s synonyms and antonyms.
  • Use the word in a sentence and illustrate the word with a drawing.
  • Organize the information you gather into a chart. Then present your words to the class.
