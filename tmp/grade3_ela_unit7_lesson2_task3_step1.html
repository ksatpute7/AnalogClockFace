“The Hen and the Apple Tree”

Tell a partner what a fable is in your own words.

Listen as your teacher reads “The Hen and the Apple Tree” aloud to see if it fits the definition of a fable. Then, Turn and Talk with your partner about what you noticed.

Read “The Hen and the Apple Tree” with a partner.

  • Write down the most important parts of the fable in the Paired Retelling Form.

  • Now retell the fable to your partner. Use the Paired Retelling Form to make sure you include all of the important parts of the fable.

  • After retelling the fable with a partner, think about these questions.

    • Why did the author write this fable?

    • What could you learn from this fable?

    • Why do authors use animals as characters in stories or fables?

  • Discuss these questions with your partner and write down your thoughts in your Notebook. Use the Animal Community Characteristics chart to help you with your answer.

Share your thoughts with the rest of the class.
