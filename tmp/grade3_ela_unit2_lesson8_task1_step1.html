Pronouns

Pronouns are useful parts of the English language. Pronouns “stand in” for nouns. The pronouns his, hers, and their show possession.

  • With a partner, read this section from Birdsong for Anna and identify the pronouns:

“One day as a drizzle fell from a gray sky, Granddad slipped on the slick wooden porch boards. His hip broke. After it healed, he decided he could no longer manage to live in the farmhouse.”
