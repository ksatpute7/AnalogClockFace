Group Venn Diagram

Meet with a small group to discuss how the The Rough-Face Girl and Cinderella are similar and different. As you discuss the stories, work together to complete a Venn diagram that compares the stories.

Discuss these questions in your group.

  • What was the big idea in Cinderella?
  • What was the big idea in The Rough-Face Girl?
  • How was the character Cinderella ”invisible” like the man in The Rough-Face Girl?
  • What made each of them “visible” to the ones they marry in the end of the books? 

Share your Venn diagram and your thinking.
