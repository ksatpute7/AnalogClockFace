Fable Plan and Character Profile

You have read fables that teach lessons about pride and trickery. Now think about a lesson you would want to teach someone (your audience). You might think about your own experiences, such as when you saw someone being bullied or heard someone lying and thought they should learn to be kinder or to tell the truth.

  • Choose a lesson or moral for a fable.
  • Create a character profile chart in your Notebook for your main character like the ones for King Lion and the Wolf.
  • What character traits will your main character possess?
  • Choose an animal that fits with your character profile.

Submit your plan to your teacher before the next lesson.
