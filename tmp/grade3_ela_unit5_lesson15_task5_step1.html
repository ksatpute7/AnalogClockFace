Plan Your Cinderella Story

Use the Cinderella Story Graphic Organizer to plan your Cinderella story.

After you have planned your Cinderella story, think about doing the following tasks to make your story even more fun to read!

  • Add dialogue between your characters.
  • Share your story with a partner and receive feedback.
  • Revise your story. Are there any changes you want to make?
  • Think of a fun, descriptive title for your story.
  • Do your characters have names? What names fit your characters best?
