Grammar and Language: Plural Nouns

To make regular nouns plural, you add an -s or an -es to the end of the word.

Irregular nouns don’t follow these rules. Here are some examples of irregular nouns:

  • man/men
  • woman/women
  • foot/feet
  • child/children
  • mouse/mice
  • goose/geese
  • tooth/teeth
