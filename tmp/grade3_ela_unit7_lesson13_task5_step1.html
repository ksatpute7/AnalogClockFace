The Tale of Despereaux

Continue to read The Tale of Despereaux by Kate DiCamillo. You may read this independently or with a partner.

You should read Chapters 19–36 by Lesson 15.

As you read, think about the following questions.

  • At the beginning of Chapter 23, the narrator says, “Every action, reader, no matter how small, has a consequence.”
  • How does this apply so far to Despereaux’s parents?
  • How does this apply to Princess Pea?
