Poetry Language Study

What if your teacher said, “Your books please now do quickly take and with your pencil writing make?”

Even though the sentence sounds strange, you can probably understand it. Your teacher is saying that you should take out your books and begin to write.

A lot of poetry has a structure or organization that is different from what you see in fiction or informational text. The sentences may look or sound different, but you can still understand them.
