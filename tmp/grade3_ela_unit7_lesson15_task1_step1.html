Opening
Project Discussion

Work in small groups and discuss the following questions.

  • What are the different topics your group members are using so far in their projects?
  • What are the different text structures your group members are using so far in their projects?
  • How many facts, definitions, and details have your group members gathered on your topic?

Write down what you and your partners are thinking.

Then, share with the class the important ideas you and your group discussed.
