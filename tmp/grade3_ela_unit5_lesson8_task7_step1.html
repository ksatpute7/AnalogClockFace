Tell Your Story

Begin by thinking about the story you will tell.

  • What do you want the audience to know about you?
  • How you will make sure your audience learns about you from your story?

Use Narrative Map: Story From Your Life to help you plan your story.

Then meet with a partner and take turns telling your stories. Give each other feedback and tell what you learned about him or her from the story. Remember to refer to the Characteristics of Good Storytelling chart when telling your partner how he or she succeeded at storytelling and also what needs to be worked on.
