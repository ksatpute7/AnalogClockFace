Vocabulary: Frayer Model

In this task, you will work with the six vocabulary words from The Rough-Face Girl:

  • haughtily

  • insisted

  • stammered

  • desperately

  • feverishly

  • awesome

  • Watch and listen as your teacher shows you how to gather information from the text about what these words mean.
  • Then, using the Frayer model, work with a group to define the word you were assigned.
  • Next, look the word up in a dictionary to check on its meaning.
