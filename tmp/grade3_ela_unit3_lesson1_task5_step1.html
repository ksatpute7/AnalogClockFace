In How do Animals Adapt?, reread the sections “Why do animals adapt?,” “Adapting to the seasons,” “Taking a long trip,” “Staying warm,” and “Keeping cool.”

On the first page of text, the author writes that animals adapt to find food, escape danger, defend themselves and their young, and adjust to hot and cold temperatures.

  • Work with a partner to find examples of each of these adaptations in the text. One partner will need to have the text open on the tablet and the other partner will need to have the matrix open. Both partners need to contribute facts to complete the matrix.
  • Using these examples, complete the Adaptation Matrix.
  • Be sure to explain how the adaptation helps the animal stay alive.
