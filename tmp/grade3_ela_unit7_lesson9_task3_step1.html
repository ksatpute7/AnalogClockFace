"A Day For Tigers"

The first text you will read shows a problem where it is the animal—a tiger—that needs protection. Before you read, discuss with your class reasons why people kill animals.  

Your teacher will show you how to use text coding when you read. Text coding is a way you can keep track of your thoughts and questions while you read.

Then, read along to yourself as your teacher reads aloud.

  • Annotate the text. Mark important facts and ideas from “A Day for Tigers.”
  • Code the text using the tools your teacher shows you.
