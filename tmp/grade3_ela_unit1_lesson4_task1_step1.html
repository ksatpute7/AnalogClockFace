You are going to help your teacher create a rubric for Independent Reading behaviors or habits.

Review the Independent Reading chart that you have been creating with your classmates.

Next, help your teacher to make the rubric.

Your rubric will have three categories.

  • “Meets the Standards” means that you are doing what you should be doing.
What should go under “Meets the Standards”?
  • “Working Toward the Standards” means you could use a little improvement, but you could do it with a little more work.
What should go under “Working Toward the Standards” on the rubric?
  • “Needs Support” means you should get help from your teacher and classmates so you can improve.
