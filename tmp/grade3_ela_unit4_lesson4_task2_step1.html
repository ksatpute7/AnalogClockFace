Characters’ Actions, Thoughts, and Feelings

Now, you are going to study Gram’s Angel Food Cake to see how an author develops the plot and reveals information about the characters by describing a character’s actions, thoughts, and feelings.

Read through Gram’s Angel Food Cake with your partner, looking for examples of Abi’s actions in the beginning, middle, and end of the narrative. Since actions are things a character does, look for verbs.

  • Record your thoughts on the Describing Abi’s Actions, Thoughts, and Feelings form.
  • Now, Turn and Talk with your partner about the following questions:
    • How does Abi feel during the narrative?
    • What does she think?
    • Does what she feel and think change during the narrative? Why?
