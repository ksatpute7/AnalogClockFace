Extinct Animals

We now understand that some animals can cause major problems in a community of other animals or people. It is important that we know what problems could happen with certain animals and how we can go about solving the problems.

  • Make a three-column chart to help organize your ideas. Look at the sample chart your teacher made as an example. Listen as your teacher discusses extinct animals.
  • Jot down different problems these animals have in a community in the first column of your chart. If you notice any causes for these problems, write those in the second column.
