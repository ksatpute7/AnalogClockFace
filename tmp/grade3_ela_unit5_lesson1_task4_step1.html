Understanding Tone

In Crow Boy, the feeling of the story changed when the students started thinking of Chibi in a different way. The feeling of a story is also called tone. Authors create the tone of the story by carefully choosing the words they use.

Think about the tone at the beginning of Crow Boy. Think about the tone at the end of Crow Boy.

  • What words did the author use to create the tone?
