Interview Report

When you receive a reply from your community member, you will begin to write your report on that person.

Review the Grade 3 Informational Writing Rubric with your class.

You can work with your partner, but each of you must produce your own piece of writing. Your teacher will show you how to do this and then you can give it a try.

  • Your report should have two paragraphs: one describing the community member’s work and one telling about how that person’s job helps the school to run smoothly.
  • As you draft your report, use the Grade 3 Informational Writing Rubric to guide your work and make any needed revisions or additions.
