Grammar and Language: Function of Verbs

Some verbs show physical actions, like run and go.

Some verbs show mental actions, like think and hope.

Some verbs show a state of being, like is and was.

  • Identify the verbs in the following sentences. Does the verb show a physical action, mental action, or state of being?
    • Arturo cleans his kitchen.
    • Emma often thinks about her trip to Brazil.
    • After the soccer game, Alison was tired.
