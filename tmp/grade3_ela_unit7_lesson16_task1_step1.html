Opening
Read and Discuss

Reread "A Day for Tigers" and "Wild or Wrangled" with a partner.

Suggestion: You may read one article and your partner may read the other.

Think about the following questions while you read.

  • What patterns do you see with solving problems with animals in a certain community?
  • What parts of the texts helped you understand how the problems were solved?

Write down what you and your partner are thinking, and then share your thoughts with the class. Use information from the texts to support your thinking.
