Week 12, Day 3: Magnetic Letters

The words this week are challenging. Today, you will work with a partner to practice spelling them.

  • Exchange spelling lists with your partner.
  • Open your Magnetic Letters.
  • One partner will read the first word on the list while the other builds the word with Magnetic Letters.
  • Check the word together.
  • Switch roles and repeat the process.
  • Continue until you complete your lists.
