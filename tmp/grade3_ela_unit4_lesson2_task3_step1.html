Introduction: Gram’s Angel Food Cake

Now you will learn one way to write a strong introduction, focusing on a main character and describing him or her through words and actions.

First, reread the introduction to Gram’s Angel Food Cake. Then Turn and Talk with a partner about your responses to the following questions. You and your partner need to annotate the text with your responses.

  • What features are included in the introduction?
  • How is Gram described? Find examples in the text.
  • What do you learn about Gram as a character?

Share with the class specific examples of your annotations.
