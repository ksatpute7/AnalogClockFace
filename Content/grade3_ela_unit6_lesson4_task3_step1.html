Linking Words

Have you included any linking words in your report?

  • Highlight any linking words that you already have in your report, and add some now if you do not have any.
  • Look for short sentences that can be joined using linking words like but or so. Or, find sentences that could begin with a linking word. For example, if you have written more than one sentence about your animal’s characteristics, you could start one of your sentences with also or another.

Share your linking words with a classmate.
