Sentence Deconstruction

Did you know that you can move parts of sentences around to make them more interesting and easier to understand? See how this works as your teacher demonstrates this process.

Read this sentence from the H page of What is a Community? from A to Z:

“By learning about the history of your country and community, you can understand more about your way of life today.”

Read the sentence again. This time, read the part after the comma first and then read the first part of the sentence.

  • Now rearrange this sentence in a way that makes sense:

    When handling a hot pan, it is best to use a potholder.
