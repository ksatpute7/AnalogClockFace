Lesson 22

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, students will finish reading “Watching in the Wild” and will watch a video called Mammals: Chimp Tools.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers
  • Jane Goodall and the Chimps—What They Did chart
  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Watch the Mammals: Chimp Tools video to prepare yourself to lead the class discussion.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 2 and 4.
