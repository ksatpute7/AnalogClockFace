LESSON 4: Adding Large Numbers

Lesson Overview

Students will solve a problem that requires them to add 2 three-digit numbers with a sum in the thousands. Then, they will practice adding three-digit numbers.

Key Concepts

Students are familiar with the standard algorithm for adding whole numbers from Grade 2. In Grade 2, all the sums that they calculated were less than 1,000. In this lesson, the algorithm is extended to include sums greater than 1,000 with addends of up to three digits. When the sum of the hundreds digits is 10 or greater, students need to regroup to form a thousand.
