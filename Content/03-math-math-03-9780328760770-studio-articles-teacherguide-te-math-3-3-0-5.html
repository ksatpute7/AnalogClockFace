LESSON 5: Twos Multiplication Table

Lesson Overview

Students construct the Twos Multiplication Table by building a group of 2 and replicating iterations of that group of 2. Students represent each of the facts in the Twos Multiplication Table by building a model, writing a multiplication equation, and filling in blanks in a sentence. Students look for patterns in the multiples of 2.

Key Concepts

Representing a situation of a single group of 2 using multiplication may not seem natural to students since the total number of children is obviously 2 if there is 1 car with 2 children. Emphasizing the connection to actual situations can help students make sense of these expressions.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Investigate groups of 2

  • Continue to discover the properties of multiplication by building the Twos Multiplication Table

  • Understand “2 people in each ride” as a single group of 2

  • Identify patterns in the multiples of 2
