Work Time

Task 7: Reader’s and Writer’s Autobiography

    • Explain that Work Time is a time for students to practice what they just learned.

    • Circulate through the room and provide assistance and feedback to students as they complete their questionnaires.

      Emerging P1.3.10b

      Monitor that all Emerging ELLs understand the meaning of the words and statements used in the questionnaire. If needed, read each question one by one, focusing on meaning-carrying words, not syntax. Probe to check for understanding. Pair students with other students who have higher proficiency with the English language, as available, to go over the questions and to respond. Offer support (including sentence frames) as needed.

      Expanding P1.3.10b

      Once you have verified that Expanding ELLs understand the meaning of the questions in the questionnaire, probe to see if they need additional support to respond (e.g., sentence frames). Encourage them to move from expanded phrases to full sentences, if possible. Allow students to work in small groups that include native English speakers.

      Bridging P1.3.10b

      Encourage Bridging ELLs to rehearse their answers orally before writing them. Monitor to make sure they are successful at answering the questions by themselves. Allow access to monolingual and bilingual dictionaries in English and in their native language. Dictionary use is an important asset for developing English language proficiency.
