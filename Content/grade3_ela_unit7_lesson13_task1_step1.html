Text Structure: Turtles & Tortoises

Authors choose the text structure that will give the reader the information in the clearest way.

Follow along as your teacher reads aloud two chapters of Turtles & Tortoises—“Turtle or Tortoise?” and “Life of a Turtle: From Egg to Adult.”

As you follow along, think about the following questions.

  • How does the author organize each section?
  • Why does the author organize the section that way?

Meet with your partner to discuss your responses to these questions, and then share your thinking with the class.
