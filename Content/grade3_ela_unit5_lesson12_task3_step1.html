Ideas Inspired by Cinderella

Strong writers take inspiration from the authors they read. You took inspiration from Taro Yashima and Patricia Polacco.

Now think about what you have read so far in Cinderella by Eleanor Fremont.

  • Is there an idea for your own story here?
  • Perhaps you have trouble getting along with your brothers or sisters, or you have so many chores it seems unfair. Do you want to do something special but you are not allowed to?
  • Do you wish something magical would solve a problem?
  • What story will you write?
