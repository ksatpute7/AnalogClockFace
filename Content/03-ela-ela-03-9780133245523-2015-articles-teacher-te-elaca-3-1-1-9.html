Lesson 9

Lesson Overview

Today, you will discuss the importance of word choice with students. You will also introduce Reading Conferences and explain their routine to the students.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers
  • Temporal Words chart
  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)
  • Bilingual dictionaries (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Locate the word choice examples in Boxes for Katje.
  • Review the “Getting to Know You” Conference Guide.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 3 and 6.
