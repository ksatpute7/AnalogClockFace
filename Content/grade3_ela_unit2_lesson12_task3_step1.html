Practice With Your Group

You are going to perform one of the poems with a group of your classmates.

Sit with your group and decide the following.

  • Will you perform Judith Viorst’s version of “If I Were in Charge of the World” or the class’s version of “If I Were in Charge of the World”?
  • How will you perform the poem?
    • Read the whole poem together as a group.
    • Have each group member read one stanza.
    • Have one group member read the first line of a stanza and another group member finish the stanza.
  • Decide on a plan that is all your own. You might put the poem to music, illustrate the poem, or turn the poem into a play.

Practice performing with your group.
