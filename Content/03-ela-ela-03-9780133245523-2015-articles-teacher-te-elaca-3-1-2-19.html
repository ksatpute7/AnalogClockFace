Lesson 19

Lesson Overview

Students will learn what a Response Group is and will participate in a Response Group to give and receive feedback on their interview reports. They will also begin to construct their Writing Portfolio.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers for Response Group Procedures chart
  • Index cards
  • Materials to provide sentence frames
  • Spelling Strategies chart
  • Editing Strategies Checklist

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • For each Response Group, create an index card of possible questions to ask other group members.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Task 7.
