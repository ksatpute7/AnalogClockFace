Task 2: Sentence Deconstruction

    • In this task, students will unpack the meaning of a sentence from Boxes for Katje.

    • Display this sentence: “Then, as Postman Kleinhoonte sang, the students danced, and Mrs. deLand sniffled, Katje divided the sugar.”

    • Tell students that there are four things happening in this sentence.

      • What are the four things?
      • When are these things happening. One after another? (no, all at once)
      • What is the key word that lets the reader know they are happening all at once. (as)

    • Tell students that “as” can mean “while” or “when” and can be used to show actions happening simultaneously. “As” is a conjunction used to join ideas together. In this sentence it is being used to join together the four things that are happening.

    • Ask students if they could write this sentence in another way and keep the same meaning.

      • Have students work with a partner to rewrite the sentence in their Notebook.
