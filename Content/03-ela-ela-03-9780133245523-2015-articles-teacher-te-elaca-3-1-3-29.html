Lesson 29

Lesson Overview

This lesson will get at the heart of book discussions. What makes a quality book discussion? How do we make sure that we come to our Book Club sessions prepared? The lesson will ensure that Book Clubs are ready for their first meeting.

Materials

  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Prepare the chart of conversation prompts for Book Clubs.
  • Form Book Club groups based on student preferences. There should be four or five students in each Book Club.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 2 and 5.
