Lesson 26

Lesson Overview

This lesson introduces Guided Reading to students and helps to clarify the expectations for students when they are not in a Guided Reading Group.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers
  • Decodable texts: Leona’s Poem and Joyce Paints
  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Familiarize yourself with the grammar and language focus in this lesson and the vocabulary words.
  • Peruse the decodable texts.
  • Prepare to add a new expectation to the Independent Reading chart. Read the background notes for Guided Reading to prep yourself for class discussion.
  • Use the information you’ve gained from conferences, as well as other observations/assessments you have made, to form small homogeneous groups of four to six students for Guided Reading.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 5 and 7.
