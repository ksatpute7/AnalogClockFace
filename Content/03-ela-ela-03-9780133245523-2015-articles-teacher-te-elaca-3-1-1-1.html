Lesson 1

Lesson Overview

Today, you will introduce the concept of community to the class. You will also do an App Walk to familiarize students with the Pearson System of Courses app. This is also a time to introduce students to the routines they will be following in class every day.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers
  • Materials to provide sentence frames

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Complete the Reader’s and Writer’s Autobiography prior to class so that you can share it with the class.
  • Review the article Classroom Conversations.
  • Familiarize yourself with the weekly vocabulary words, the phonics skills of the week, and the decodable texts available to use with specific student groups.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 1 and 3.
