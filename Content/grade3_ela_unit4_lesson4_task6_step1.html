Revising Your Cold Write

So far in this lesson, you have learned two important strategies for developing your narratives:

1) Writing descriptions of characters’ actions, thoughts, and feelings

2) Writing purposeful dialogue

  • Now, you will revise your Cold Write narratives using the new strategies that you have learned. Use the class charts, the graphic organizer you completed, and Gram’s Angel Food Cake to help you. You can check off your ideas on your graphic organizer as you work. Look for places where you could add in purposeful dialogue.

As you revise, remember to punctuate dialogue correctly.
