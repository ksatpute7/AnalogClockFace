Patterns in Skip-Counting by 5s and 10s

  • Work on your own.

    • Open the Six Hundreds Chart.
    • Choose whether to skip-count by 5s or 10s. Your skip-count number is your start number.
    • Shade your start number.
    • As you skip-count, shade the numbers you say.
    • Describe the patterns in your chart.

  • Work with your partner.

    • Compare your work with your partner’s work. Challenge your partner to explain the patterns clearly and completely.
  • If you picked skip-counting by 5s and are finding it difficult, try skip-counting by 10s instead.
  • How does shading help you see the skip-counting patterns?
  • Do the rows or columns help you see the skip-counting pattern?
