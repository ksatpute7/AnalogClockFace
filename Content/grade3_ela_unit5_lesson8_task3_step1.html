Write Your Opinion

It is time to write a reading response to one of the books you have read so far in this unit. You can write about the one you liked the best or liked the least.

  • Write your opinion about either Chicken Sunday or Crow Boy.

Remember the things your teacher included in an opinion piece.

Refer to the Grade 3 Opinion Writing Rubric as you write. Your opinion piece should include the following.

  • A brief introduction, including the title of the book
  • A clear statement of an opinion and the reason for the opinion
  • Examples from the text to support the opinion
  • A logical order to the piece
  • A conclusion that might restate the opinion
