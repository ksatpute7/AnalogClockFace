Lesson 23

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, students focus on the opinion genre by reading “Pet Primates: Why Not?” Then they play “So You Want to Be a Millionaire?”

Materials

  • Play money for Task 5 (optional)
  • Chart paper for the “So You Want to Be a Millionaire?” rules (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Review the Grade 3 Opinion Writing Rubric.
  • Look over the annotated version of “Pet Primates: Why Not?”
  • Review the questions and answers for “So You Want to Be a Millionaire?” in Task 6.
  • Write the rules of the game “So You Want to Be a Millionaire?” on chart paper, and post the chart so ELLs can see it.
