Colors and Feelings

Illustrations in a text can show feelings.

Reread the class version of “If I Were in Charge of the World” with a partner.

  • What color you would connect to each stanza of the poem?

Support your ideas with lines from the stanzas and what you think Anna is trying to say in the stanzas. Explain why you would connect the feelings you get from the stanza with a particular color.

Share your ideas with the rest of the class.
