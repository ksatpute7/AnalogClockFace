Task 2: Fluency: Expression and Intonation

    • Have students turn to the V page in What is a Community? from A to Z, which they read for homework.

    • Tell students to follow along in the text while you read it aloud, modeling expression and intonation, such as where there are exclamation points. Show how you pause briefly at commas and more fully at periods.

    • Next, have students read the page with you.

    • Then have them continue to reread the page without you until they read with expression and intonation.

    • If possible, use technology to have students record their reading and play it back to listen for expression and intonation.
