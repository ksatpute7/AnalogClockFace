Lesson 8

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you introduce the concept of temporal words to students. Students reread Boxes for Katje and then you will lead the class in finding temporal words in the text.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers for Temporal Words chart
  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Create a pre-selected list of student groupings based on their language needs for Task 2.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 2 and 4.
