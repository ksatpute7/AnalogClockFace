Work Time

Task 4: App Walk

    • Take your students on an App Walk and then give them time to freely explore. Allowing students time to “play” will enable them to locate items more easily and provide them with knowledge of what’s available in the app.

    • Introduce students to the online dictionary and recommend that they use this resource often.

      Monitor the participation level of students with disabilities. They often work at a slower pace and may be reluctant to share. Providing more wait-time than is typical may help them to feel more comfortable participating.

    • At the end of this session, ask students what questions they have and answer them.
