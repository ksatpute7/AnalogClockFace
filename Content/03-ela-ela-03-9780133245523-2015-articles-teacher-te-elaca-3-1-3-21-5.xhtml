Task 5: Jane Goodall Discussion

    • Lead a class discussion about what students have learned from the reading. Have students keep the text open and ask them to provide evidence from the text to make their point. With your encouragement, students will develop the habit of returning to the text and discussing the text with the book or article in hand.

      Invite reluctant students to share even though they may work at a slower pace and need more wait-time than might be expected. Providing them with advance notice that they will be called on to share and helping them prepare to do so will support them in becoming more comfortable sharing over time.
