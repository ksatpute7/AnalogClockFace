Task 11: Author’s Chair

    • Explain that during the Author’s Chair routine, one chair is reserved for the student sharing his or her written work with the class. Other students can be seated around this chair, or perhaps on the floor. The student author may answer questions after the reading, but it is up to you whether to include this part.

    • Ask two or three students to read their Independent Reading rephrasings from their Notebook.

      Before students with disabilities participate in sharing with the whole group, have a quick conference with them to make sure they are prepared. This will give them confidence to participate in the larger setting.
