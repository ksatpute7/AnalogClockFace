Task 7: Book Talk

    • Have students meet in groups of three or four and give a brief Book Talk about the book they are reading for Independent Reading. Remind them that in a Book Talk you give the title, author, and a brief summary.

      Monitor students’ understanding of ideas in their book as well as their ability to keep up with their reading. Assign them specific tasks to accomplish if they are falling behind or need to focus on a specific concept or skill.

    • Students should also share at least one response to the following:

      • Point out a favorite part or passage and explain why that’s a favorite.
      • Read a favorite line or description and explain why that’s a favorite.
      • Why do you believe your siblings, parents or guardians might enjoy this book or not?
      • Why do you believe your teacher might like this book … or not?
