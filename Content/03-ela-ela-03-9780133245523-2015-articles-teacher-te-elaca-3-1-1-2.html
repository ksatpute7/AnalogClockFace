Lesson 2

Lesson Overview

The goal of this lesson is to help students select books for Independent Reading at an appropriate level of challenge. Explain to students that you will help them look for books that they want to read and books that they can read.

Materials

  • A book that you are currently reading
  • Chart paper and markers
  • Decodable texts: A Winter Picnic and Summer Picnic
  • Five-Finger Rule handout
  • Materials to provide sentence frames

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Read the Independent Reading Primer and Choosing a Just-Right Book for more information about the topic of Independent Reading.
  • Gather the decodable books listed in this lesson if you plan to use these with particular groups of students.
  • Create a handout of the Five-Finger Rule.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Task 5.
