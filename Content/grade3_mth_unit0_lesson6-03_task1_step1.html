Work With Your Notebook

Go to your Notebook, and play with the drawing area.

Try different ways to use your Notebook.

  • Write with a stylus or your finger.
  • Change the width of lines and the color.
  • Use the keypad to type text.
  • Add media by taking a picture or a video.
  • Choose a background from the templates.

Be prepared to talk about at least one of the features.
