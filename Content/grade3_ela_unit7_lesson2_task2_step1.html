Zooming in on Language

Read the following sentences from “King Lion and the Beetle.”

“King Lion looked in the mirror. ‘What a beautiful and noble creature I am,’ he said. ‘I will go forth to show my devoted subjects that their leader is every inch a king!’”

  • If the author wanted to include the introductory phrase “one day” in the first sentence, how might he have written the sentence? Try writing the sentence that way in your Notebook.
  • In your Notebook, write a sentence of your own that begins with an introductory phrase that tells when an event is taking place.
  • In your writing today, try starting with a phrase that tells when the action in the sentence is taking place.
