Select Your Favorite Book

Part of becoming a strong reader is thinking about your own likes and dislikes about what you read.

Today, you will select your favorite book from this unit, and then write about why it was your favorite and what it taught you about community.

Listen as your teacher reviews the elements for writing an opinion.

  • A brief introduction and the title of the book
  • A clear statement of an opinion
  • An organizational structure that lists reasons
  • Examples from the text to support the opinion
  • Use of linking words and phrases to connect opinion and reasons
  • A concluding statement or section that might restate the opinion
  • Begin writing your opinion piece.
