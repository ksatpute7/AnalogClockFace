Vocabulary Review

Play a quick game of Either-Or to review some of this unit’s vocabulary words. Your teacher will read the following sentences aloud. Choose the best word for each.

  • The Rough-Face Girl’s sisters (stammered, snickered) when they were asked questions by the sister of the Invisible Being.
  • My mother (insisted, taunted) that I return home by dinner.
  • The roller coaster ride was (arrogant, awesome).
  • The woman (haughtily, desperately) tried to finish the race ahead of her friend.
  • The sandpaper felt (soft, coarse) against my skin.
