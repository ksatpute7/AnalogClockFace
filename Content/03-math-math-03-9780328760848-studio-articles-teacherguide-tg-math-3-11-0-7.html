LESSON 7: Weighty Problems

Lesson Overview

Students solve multiplication and division word problems involving measuring weight in grams and kilograms. They complete two Self Check tasks involving volume and weight

Key Concepts

Students continue to solve word problems involving weight and volume. The problems in this lesson can be most efficiently solved using multiplication and division.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Solve word problems involving measuring weight in grams and kilograms using all four operations.

Materials

  • Pan balance and weights for Challenge Problem (at least one for every four students)

Preparation

Set up pan balances and weights in a designated area so students can come up and weigh objects for the Challenge Problem.
