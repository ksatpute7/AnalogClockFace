LESSON 6: Subtracting From 1,000

Lesson Overview

Students will explore methods of subtracting a whole number from 1,000. Students will complete a Self Check task.

Content Standards

3.NBT—Number and Operations in Base Ten

Use place value understanding and properties of operations to perform multi-digit arithmetic.

2. Fluently add and subtract within 1000 using strategies and algorithms based on place value, properties of operations, and/or the relationship between addition and subtraction.

Key Concepts

Subtracting from 1,000 using the subtraction algorithm can be tricky for students. Because there are 0s in the tens place and the hundreds place of the minuend, students must go all the way to the thousands place to begin regrouping. They must regroup 1 thousand to get 10 hundreds, regroup 1 of those hundreds to get 10 tens, and then regroup 1 of those tens to get 10 ones. After all this regrouping is done, they can subtract place by place.

Students may find other methods—such as subtracting one part of the subtrahend at a time, or the constant difference method—more efficient than the algorithm.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Understand methods for subtracting from 1,000

  • Subtract whole numbers from 1,000
