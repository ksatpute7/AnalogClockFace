LESSON 11: More Two-Step Problems

Lesson Overview

Students will apply what they have learned throughout the unit to solve a two-step word problem of their choice. They will then prepare a presentation that shows their work.

Key Concepts

In this lesson, students will choose one of four given word problems and find the solution. Solving the problems requires applying the ideas they have learned and practiced throughout this unit.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Represent two-step word problems with diagrams and equations

  • Estimate solutions to problems

  • Apply the correct order of operations and the inverse relationship between operations to solve an equation with an unknown

  • Use the inverse relationships between operations to check solutions to equations
