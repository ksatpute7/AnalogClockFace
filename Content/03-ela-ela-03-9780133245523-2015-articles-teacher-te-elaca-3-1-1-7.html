Lesson 7

Lesson Overview

Today’s lesson involves a deeper analysis of Boxes for Katje. Students reread portions of Boxes for Katje and discuss the two communities in the story.

Materials

  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Reread Boxes for Katje and review the “big idea” to prepare for class discussion.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 2, 3, and 4.
