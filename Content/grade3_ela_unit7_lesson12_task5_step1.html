Work Organization

Review the information you have collected for your project so far.

Decide what topics or sections you will use to organize your informational text about the animal you chose.

  • It may be helpful to visually organize the information into topics or sections using index cards or sticky notes. Remember, one section should be about the problem that animal has in a certain community.
  • Once you have the sections or topics selected for your project, show them to your teacher for feedback. If you have already received feedback from your teacher, you may start writing the sections that will be included in your poster.

This project should be completed by Lesson 16.
