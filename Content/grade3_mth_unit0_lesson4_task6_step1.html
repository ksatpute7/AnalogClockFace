Make Connections
  • Take notes about how your classmates’ skip-counting patterns were the same as and different from your patterns.

As your classmates present, ask questions such as:

  • How are the patterns the same when skip-counting by 5s and 10s?
  • How are the patterns different when skip-counting by 5s and 10s?
  • How would the hundred chart look different if you started skip-counting at a different number, such as 13?
