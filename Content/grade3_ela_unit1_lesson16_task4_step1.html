Revision Strategies

What word do you see within the word revision?

When you revise, you look at your writing again and make changes based on your new vision of the piece.

Revision is the opportunity to consider options for clarifying meaning and improving the craft of your writing. Think about the things you have done to revise, or to improve, the content of your writing.

When you have a few ideas about what you can do to improve the content of your writing and to make your meaning clearer, indicate you are ready by raising your hand.

Work with the class to make a list of revision strategies.
