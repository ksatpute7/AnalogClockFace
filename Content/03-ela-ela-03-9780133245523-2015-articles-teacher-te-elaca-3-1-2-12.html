Lesson 12

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, students will learn how to read with a partner.

Materials

  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Review Partner Reading to help set up and sustain reading partnerships in your classroom.
  • Determine how you want to pair students for reading partners in advance of the lesson. It is recommended that students of similar ability be paired with one another so that pairs aren’t placed in a “teacher-student” relationship where the stronger reader takes on a tutorial role.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Task 4.
