“The Land of Nod” Discussion

Use the following questions to discuss “The Land of Nod.”

  • Why does the child have to go all by himself to the Land of Nod?
  • What are the “frightening sights"?
  • When the child says, “Try as I like to find the way / I never can get back by day,” what does he mean?
  • Does he remember what he hears and sees in the Land of Nod when he wakes up?

Listen as your classmates share and add your own thoughts.
