What Is a Memoir?

  • Can you think of a word that sounds like memoir?

  • What do you think the word memoir might mean?

A memoir is a memory someone has about his or her life, told as a story. Birdsong for Anna is a memoir of the author Betty Ochester.

One way to learn how this kind of story is written is by making a map of the story. 

Listen as your teacher shows you how to complete a narrative map.

Work with your partner to complete the Narrative Map: Birdsong for Anna.

  • Use the map the class created together to get started.
  • Add the events from the text in the order they happened.

Go back to the story as often as you like to find the information you need.
