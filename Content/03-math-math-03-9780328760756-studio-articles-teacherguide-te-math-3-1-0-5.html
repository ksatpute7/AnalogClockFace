LESSON 5: Time Intervals

Lesson Overview

Solve math stories about time. Complete a Self Check task.

Content Standards

Solve problems involving measurement and estimation of intervals of time, liquid volumes, and masses of objects.

1. Tell and write time to the nearest minute and measure time intervals in minutes. Solve word problems involving addition and subtraction of time intervals in minutes, e.g., by representing the problem on a time line.

Key Concepts

Students use addition and subtraction to solve word problems involving intervals of time in minutes. Students can apply the same strategies they have used to solve addition and subtraction word problems to these time word problems. They complete a Self Check task.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Solve word problems involving addition and subtraction of time intervals in minutes

  • Complete a Self Check task

Materials

  • Classroom analog clock, optional
