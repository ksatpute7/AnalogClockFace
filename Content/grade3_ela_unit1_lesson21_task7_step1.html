Book Talk

Meet in a small group and give a brief Book Talk about the book you have been reading during Independent Reading. Remember to name the title and author, and give a brief summary (without giving away the ending).

Share one or more responses to the following.

  • Point out a favorite part or passage and explain why that is a favorite.

  • Read a favorite line or description and explain why that is a favorite.

  • Why do you believe your sibling, parents or guardians might enjoy this book or not?

  • Why do you believe your teacher might like this book … or not?
