Introduction: Boxes for Katje

Now you will learn a second way to write a strong introduction, introducing the setting by describing details of time and place.

First, reread the introduction to Boxes for Katje.

The author focuses on a different feature in the introduction. Think about the following questions.

  • What feature is focused on?
  • Where in the text does the author give the reader information about the setting? Remember to look for both time and place.
  • Now Turn and Talk with your partner and annotate the text with your responses to the questions.
