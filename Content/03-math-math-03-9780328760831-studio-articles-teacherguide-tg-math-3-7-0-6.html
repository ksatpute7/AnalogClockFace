LESSON 6: How Many in Each?

Lesson Overview

Students chant along with the multiplication animation to review the sixes. They then compare using division to find equal shares with using division to find the number of equal groups.

Key Concepts

In previous lessons, students used division to find equal shares of a quantity and to divide a quantity into an equal number of groups of a specified size. In this lesson, students are asked to compare and contrast these two kinds of division.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Compare using division to find equal shares with division to find the number of equal groups

  • Understand how sharing division and grouping division are alike and how they are different
