Work Time

Task 6: Partner and Independent Reading

    • Tell students that they have two tasks to do during Work Time .

      • Read the page for the letter D in What is a Community? from A to Z. Students can read with a partner for this task. Let students talk for a few minutes about ways in which their community has changed in the past year.
      • Then read independently for 20 minutes. Remind them to record their reading in their Reading Logs and to rate their Independent Reading.

    • Once students have settled into their tasks, continue conferring. Limit the time for each conference to about 7 minutes. You’ll get to three students today.

    Decodables

    Decodable texts are available for students who might benefit from reading texts with more phonics support.

    • This lesson’s titles are Teaching Bell to Behave and Miss Kline.

    • Follow this suggested sequence when using the decodable books:

      • Have students read the high frequency words.
      • Have students read the title and preview the text.
      • Pair students for reading and listen as they read. Have students read the entire story switching readers after each page. Have partners reread the story. Make sure students are monitoring their reading.
      • Have students discuss the story including the characters, setting, and events.
      • Encourage students to reread and record their reading, listening to be sure the reading is fluent.
