Task 5: Author’s Chair

    • Remind students that Author’s Chair is the term used for the practice of having student writers share their writing with the class. Emphasize that it is important at this stage in their work because it gives the listening students a chance to ask questions of the student author and to celebrate each other’s writing.

    • Ask three to five students to share how they edited their writing today. You may want them to give “before and after” examples of a change they made as a model for other students.

    • Ask students to reflect on the editing process using the sentence frame:

      One editing strategy I used is _____.
