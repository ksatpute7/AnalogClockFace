Task 5: Community Reflection

    • Conduct a discussion about each school worker’s importance in the school community as a whole. How does each member contribute to the common good?

      Emerging P1.3.11

      Begin the whole class community reflection by posting the discussion question:

      • How does each member contribute to the common good?

      Underline the phrase “common good” and offer the definition. Restate the question using simpler words and examples. Have Emerging ELLs take a few minutes to think about the answer before sharing with a partner. As students share, encourage them to support their opinions by providing good reasons and evidence from the interview reports.

      Expanding P1.3.11

      Define “importance” and “common good.” Post the discussion question:

      • How does each member contribute to the common good?

      Have students think about the answer before sharing with a partner. As students share, encourage students to support their opinions by providing good reasons and increasingly detailed evidence from the interview reports.

      Bridging P1.3.11
