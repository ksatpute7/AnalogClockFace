Partner Share

Meet with a partner and discuss the following questions.

  • How do you think the people in the kingdom really felt about King Lion?
  • How did you know they might feel that way? What in the text makes you think so?

Highlight words and phrases in the text to support your ideas. Write down what you and your partner are thinking, then share with the class some of your ideas. Use information from the text to support your thinking.
