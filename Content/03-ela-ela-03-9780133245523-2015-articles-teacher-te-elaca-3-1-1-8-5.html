Task 5: Independent Reading

    • Have students read independently for 20 minutes, record what they read in their Reading Log, and rate their Independent Reading.

    • Have them look for how the author of their independent text used temporal words and phrases.

    • Do drop-in conferences with students about their reading.

      Before the Whole Group Share, help students to identify ideas from their notes and discussions that are appropriate to contribute to the group discussion.
