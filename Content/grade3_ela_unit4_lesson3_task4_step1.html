Create a Logical Sequence

Now, look at your list of events and review your Cold Write. Think about the following questions.

  • Do you think your events are in the proper sequence? Do you need to change the order so that your narrative makes more sense to your reader?
  • Use your list of events to tell the story of your narrative aloud to your partner. Use your partner’s feedback to help you revise the order of your events if necessary.
  • As you were telling the story, were any important events missing? Do you need to add an event or two to your list? Add any missing events now.
  • Are there some events that are unnecessary or unimportant that you could take out? Remove any unnecessary events now.
