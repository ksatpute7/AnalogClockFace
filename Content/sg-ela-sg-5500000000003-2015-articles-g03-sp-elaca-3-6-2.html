Day 2: Phonics Review

Provide a quick review of the phonics covered in this unit.

  • Have students complete the following tasks in their Notebook.

  • Divide these words into syllables: lesson, suggest, winter.

  • Find the root word for each word: families, pencils, wishes.

  • Make these words plural: daisy, dish, plant.

  • Add -ed to these words: drop, clean, skip.

  • Add -est to these words: pretty, rich, light.

When students have completed their work, go over their responses as a class.
