Gallery Walk

Walk around the classroom to view the posters your classmates created. Use a sticky note to provide some type of feedback that could include the following.

  • A question about a section or topic. (For example, “The poster says that tigers are carnivores. If they eat plants, will they get sick?”)
  • Something you learned. (For example, “I never knew that tigers only live in Asia. I thought tigers also lived in Africa.”)
  • A feature you found helpful. (For example, “The map helped me better understand where you can find different types of tigers.”)

Leave the sticky note near the author’s desk.
