Lesson 6

Lesson Overview

Today, you will introduce students to their first Common Read of the year.

Materials

  • Decodable texts: Frogs, Flies, and Foxes and Jon and Jill
  • Chart paper and markers
  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Read the description of Common Reads (both the teacher and student versions).
  • Prepare for the Common Read by reviewing the Boxes for Katje: Text Base Annotation in advance of the lesson. It can be found on the Teacher Support site.
  • Familiarize yourself with the phonics skills, the grammar and language focus, and the vocabulary words.
  • Peruse the decodable texts available.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 4 and 5.
