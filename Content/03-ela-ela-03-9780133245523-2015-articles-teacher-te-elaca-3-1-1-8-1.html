Opening

Task 1: Homework Review

    • This task will help students to apply the plurals phonics skill in the context of decoding.

    • Ask students what words they found in Boxes for Katje as part of their homework assignment.

    • Ask students how they would pluralize the following words from Boxes for Katje: kiss, box, stomach, cupboard, scarf, business, tulip.

    • Point out that the plural of stomach isn’t stomaches. Tell students:

      • It’s what’s known as an exception to the rule. If the -ch ending is pronounced with a /k/ sound, you add -s rather than -es, as in stomach, epoch.
