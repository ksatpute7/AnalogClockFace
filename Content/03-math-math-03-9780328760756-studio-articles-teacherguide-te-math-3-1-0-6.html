LESSON 6: Putting it Together

Lesson Overview

Sort the cards to show an elapsed time math story. Revise your work on the Self Check task.

Key Concepts

Students use what they have learned about elapsed time to complete a card sort. They match a start time, an end time, a time interval, and a student card to form a set. Students revise their work on the Self Check task based on feedback from the teacher and peers.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Critique and revise work on Self Check task

  • Complete a card sort about elapsed time

Materials

  • Classroom analog clock, optional
