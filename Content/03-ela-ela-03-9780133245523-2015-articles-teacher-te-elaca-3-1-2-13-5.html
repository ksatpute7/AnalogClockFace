Task 5: Sentence Deconstruction

    • Read aloud the sentence.

    • Focus on the words such as. Explain that such as are signal words that tell the reader an example is going to be introduced. The phrase that follows such as gives an example of a school rule.

    • Ask students:

      • What school rule is being shown as an example in this sentence? (keeping quiet while the teacher is talking)

    • Ask students to complete this sentence orally with a partner: “I prefer watching television shows such as _____ .”
