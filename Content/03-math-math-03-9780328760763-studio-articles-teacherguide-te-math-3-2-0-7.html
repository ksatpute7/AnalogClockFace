LESSON 7: Putting it Together

Lesson Overview

Students will review their work on the Self Check task and think about the questions you ask. Next, each student will work with a partner to critique samples of student work. Finally, students will revise their work on the Self Check task.

Key Concepts

Each of the pieces of work students will critique exhibits some misunderstanding or confusion about rounding, adding, or subtracting whole numbers. A good critique of each piece should do these three things:

  • Say what, if anything, is done well

  • Say what errors are in the work

  • Correct the work

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Critique and revise student work

  • Consolidate understanding of rounding, adding, and subtracting whole numbers

  • Uncover and clarify any remaining misconceptions
