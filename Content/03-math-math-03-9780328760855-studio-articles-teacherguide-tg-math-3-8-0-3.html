LESSON 3: Number Line

Lesson Overview

Learn how a number line is like a ruler. Divide 1 into equal parts and find what fraction each part is of 1.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Relate reading fractional values on a ruler to reading fractional values on a number line

  • Locate 1 on the number line

  • Find what part of 1 a given length is

  • Divide the interval from 0 to 1 into equal parts

  • Express each equal part as a fraction of 1

  • Understand that the more parts 1 is divided into, the smaller each part is and the smaller the fraction is that represents it
