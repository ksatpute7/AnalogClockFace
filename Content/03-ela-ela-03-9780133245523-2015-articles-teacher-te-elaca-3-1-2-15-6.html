Task 6: Sentence Deconstruction

    • Read the sentence aloud.

    • Ask students:

      • Do you know what the word interconnected means? Is there a part of the sentence that tells you the meaning of that word? (Yes, “joined to one another”)
      • Point out that the conjunction or can be a signal that an explanation is forthcoming.

    • Display this sentence: Barns, or buildings where farm animals are housed, are often painted red.

      • Ask students: If you didn’t know what a barn was, could the sentence explain it to you? (Yes, “buildings where farm animals are housed.”)
      • Stress the usage of the word or.
