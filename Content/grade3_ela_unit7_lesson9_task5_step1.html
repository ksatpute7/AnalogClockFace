Zooming in on Language

Read the following sentence from “A Day for Tigers.”

“When scientists tallied the result, they had a very pleasant surprise—the number of tigers in Nepal had risen dramatically, proof that the program was working.”

Turn to a partner and say what the sentence means.

The author uses a dash in this sentence. The dash emphasizes important information. How does the dash help the reader?

Try writing like the author. Open your Notebook and try adding a dash to this sentence to emphasize the important information.

  • The reasons tigers are disappearing, poaching, loss of habitat, and rising sea levels, could all be stopped.

Try using a dash to emphasize important information in your writing today.
