LESSON 10: Unit Assessment

Lesson Overview

When you are ready to have students take the end-of-unit assessment, give students the assessment passcode and ask them to go to the system tray from the top left corner of their tablet screen. Make sure all students are able to find and open the correct assessment.

The assessment will take approximately 45 minutes.

SWD: Consider how to differentiate this assessment to meet the needs of the variety of learners in your classroom. Possible accommodations during this task might include:

  • provision of extra time to complete tasks

  • use of/access to a 5 function calculator to aid with basic calculations

  • modification of values used in questions to more “friendlier” numbers

  • reduction of the number of tasks necessary to demonstrate mastery

Check to ensure that students understand the quiz questions and directions. If possible, provide multiple means of representing this information to promote student comprehension.

  • ELL: As students work on the Unit Test problems, allow ELLs to use dictionaries (monolingual and bilingual).
