Word Choice in Your Story

You are going to learn how to use language to draw your reader into the culture of the story. Watch and listen as your teacher shows you how the writers of the Cinderella stories use specific words to describe their story settings.

  • Read the following sentence from Mufaro’s Beautiful Daughters: “A long time ago, in a certain place in Africa, a small village lay across a river and half a day’s journey from a city where a great king lived.”
  • How does the author use specific words to describe the story setting?
  • Turn and Talk about what you saw your teacher do and where you might be able to use word choice to describe the setting of your story.
