Grammar and Language: What Is a Noun?

What is a noun? Listen as your teacher reviews this term.

A noun is a person, place, animal, thing, or idea.

Look at this sentence:

The girls played basketball.

Girls and basketball are nouns in this sentence.

  • Now identify the nouns in Subject Nouns and Object Nouns and annotate them to show if they are used as a subject or an object.
