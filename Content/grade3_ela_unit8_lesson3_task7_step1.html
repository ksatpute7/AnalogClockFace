Poem Annotation

Sit with your group and read your chosen poem aloud. Each member of your group should read a stanza.

  • After your group reads the poem aloud, think of the Gist for the stanza that you read. Write your Gist next to the stanza.
  • Share your Gist with the other members of your group. See if they agree with you.
  • Listen as the other members of your group read aloud their Gists.


Then share your group’s Gists with the rest of the class and listen as the other groups share theirs.
