Prefixes

In this task, you will work on four prefixes. At the end of this task, there will be some trees in your classroom!

  • With your group, draw a large tree without leaves.
  • Write the prefix your teacher assigns you on the trunk of the tree.
  • Place each of the words your teacher displays on a branch of the tree.
  • Circle or highlight the root or base of the words on the branches and then write a brief definition under each word.
