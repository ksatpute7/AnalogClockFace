Task 7: Reading Log Share

    • Ask students to share what they placed in their Reading Log today. Have each student say the name of the book he/she placed in his/her Reading Log.

    • Add “Read a variety of genres” and “Record Independent Reading progress in your Reading Log” to the Independent Reading chart.

    • Remind students that during Independent Reading time they should:

      • Focus on reading the entire time.
      • Respect other readers.
      • Read a variety of genres.
      • Record Independent Reading progress in their Reading Log.
