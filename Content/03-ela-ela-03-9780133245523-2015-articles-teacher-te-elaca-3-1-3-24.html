Lesson 24

Lesson Overview

After reviewing the elements of a strong opinion piece, students will write their own opinion piece.

Materials

  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Have the Grade 3 Opinion Writing Rubric handy.
  • Review the writing prompt.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 4 and 6.
  • Create a graphic organizer for writing the opinion piece.
