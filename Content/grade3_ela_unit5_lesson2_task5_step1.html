Book Club Preparation

It’s time to begin a new round of Book Clubs. Listen as your teacher gives a brief book talk about several books.

Then browse through the books and sign up for three in order of preference:

  1. The one you’d like to read the most

  2. Your second choice

  3. Your third choice

Remember to use the Five-Finger Rule to determine if the book you want to read is just right for you.
