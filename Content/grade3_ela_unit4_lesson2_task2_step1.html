Grade 3 Narrative Writing Rubric

You are going to use the Grade 3 Narrative Writing Rubric throughout this unit as a guide to revising your Cold Write and writing an engaging and well-organized narrative.

Find the “Introduction” section on your rubric. An introduction that earns a score of 4 introduces a character or narrator clearly and sets up the situation for the reader.

Now, look at your Cold Write.

  • Does your piece have an introduction? If not, you will need to add one. If it does, what does your introduction include? Setting? Character(s)? Jot down notes about how you might improve it.
  • Share your introduction with your partner. Listen as your partner gives you feedback based on the rubric. Jot down your partner’s feedback.
  • Now switch roles and listen as your partner shares his or her introduction. Give your partner feedback. Remember to use the language from the rubric.
