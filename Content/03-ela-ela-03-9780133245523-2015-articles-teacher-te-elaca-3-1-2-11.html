Lesson 11

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, students explore what a community is and are introduced to their next Common Read, What is a Community? from A to Z.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers
  • Decodable texts: Teaching Bell to Behave and Miss Kline

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Familiarize yourself with the annotated version of What is a Community? from A to Z.
  • Familiarize yourself with the phonics skills in this lesson, the grammar and language focus, and the vocabulary words.
  • Peruse the decodable texts.
