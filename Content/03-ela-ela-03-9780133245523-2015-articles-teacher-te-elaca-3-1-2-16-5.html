Task 5: Report Revision

    • Have students revise their work and then meet with partners for 15–20 minutes. Remind students to provide positive feedback as well as respectfully suggesting improvements and asking clarifying questions.

    • Since this is probably the students’ first attempt at revision, encourage any small steps they are taking. The important thing is that they understand the need for revisiting their writing to make it clearer and stronger.

    • Confer with students to provide additional instruction or support during the revision process. While partners are meeting, circulate and listen to their conversations and the constructive feedback they share.

      Circulate among students and advise them on what to share and let them know that you will call on them during the Closing reflection. Allow them to write down their points for discussion so that they need not rely on memory.
