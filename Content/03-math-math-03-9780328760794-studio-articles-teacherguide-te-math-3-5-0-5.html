LESSON 5: Groups of 7 and 7 Groups

Lesson Overview

Students begin memorizing the sevens by repeating a multiplication chant. Students investigate what happens to the product when the multiplicand goes up by 1 (i.e., when the group size increases by 1).

Key Concepts

When constructing the multiplication tables, students have noticed and discussed that when the multiplier goes up by 1, the product goes up by the multiplicand. That is, when the number of groups increases by 1, the total increases by the number in each group.

In this lesson, students discover that it is also true that when that when the multiplicand goes up by 1, the product goes up by the multiplier. That is, when the number of items in each group increases by 1, the total increases by the number of groups.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Begin memorizing the sevens facts

  • Understand that when the multiplicand increases by 1, the product increases by the multiplier
