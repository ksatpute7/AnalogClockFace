Task 5: Prepare a Presentation

Lesson Guide

Have students prepare a presentation explaining the patterns they noticed when skip-counting.

Encourage students to include any mistakes they made along the way rather than only their final solution.

Preparing for Ways of Thinking

Look for students who identified different patterns for the same skip-counting answers, and for students who incorrectly shaded the chart or described the patterns.
