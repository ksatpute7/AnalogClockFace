Continue to read The Tale of Despereaux by Kate DiCamillo. You may read on your own or with a partner.

As you read, think about the following question.

At the end of Chapter 12, the narrator tells the reader that “farewell” is not the word that you would like to hear from your mother as you are being led to the dungeon by two oversized mice in black hoods.

  • Why did Despereaux’s mother use that word—“farewell”? Was she sending a message?
