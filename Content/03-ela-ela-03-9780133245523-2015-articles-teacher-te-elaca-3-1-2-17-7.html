Task 7: Book Talks

    • Have students meet in groups of four and give a brief Book Talk about the book they are reading for Independent Reading. Remind them that in a Book Talk, you give the title, author, a brief summary, and tell the kind of reader you think might enjoy the book.

    • When the groups are finished, ask students if they would consider reading a book that someone in their group just spoke about. Give students time to jot down titles of books they may want to read.
