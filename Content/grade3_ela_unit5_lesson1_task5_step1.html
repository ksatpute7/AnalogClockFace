Crow Boy T-Chart

Reread Crow Boy.

You Have a Choice!

You may choose to read the text on your own or with a partner.

When you are finished reading, complete the Positive and Negative Words Form.

  • Write the positive words on one side of the form.
  • Write the negative words on the other side.

Think about how each of these words influenced what you were thinking and feeling during the reading.

Then share the words you found with the rest of the class, working with your teacher to complete a class T-chart of positive and negative words. Update your own form with any new words your classmates contribute.
