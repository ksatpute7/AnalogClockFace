Week 12, Day 2: Spelling Choice

Choose one of the following:

  • Open Sort—Begin exploring and categorizing the words in your sort by creating columns or groups of words.
  • Spelling Search—Locate words with the same pattern in reading and writing. These patterns include the following.
    • VCV pattern with open and closed syllables
    • Two syllable words that end in -le
    • Compound words
    • Consonant blends squ, spl, thr, str
    • Digraphs ph, th, ch, sh

Add the words you find to a Notebook page.

Be ready to share the results of your work with the class.
