Task 2: Vocabulary

    • Discuss the six key words students will use this week and throughout the unit, and explain that these words are critical to understanding the main points of the unit. The six words are discussion, autobiography, expectation, narrative, genre, and rubric.

    • To begin, focus on the word discussion. Ask students to define discussion in their own words. Clarify that a discussion is a conversation between two or more people.

    • Next, look at the word autobiography. Explain that an autobiography is a work you write about yourself. Point out that words like autograph and automobile share the same prefix auto, meaning self. Remind students that looking for known prefixes or word parts is a strategy for unlocking the meaning of unknown words.

    • Note that each of the four remaining words will be introduced at the point of need in the instruction.

    • Have students add discussion and autobiography to their Personal Notebook. (If they have not maintained a Personal Notebook before, provide instructions on how to do so.)
