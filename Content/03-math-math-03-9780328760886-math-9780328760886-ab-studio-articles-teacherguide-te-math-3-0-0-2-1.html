Task 1: Lesson Routines

Lesson Guide

Ask students to join you in the whole-class meeting area or to prepare their desks for a whole-class discussion.

Express your gratitude to students for their effort during the last lesson and their successes in following your instructions about getting to the whole-class meeting area.

Refer students to the Math Routines poster that you prepared.

Tell students that each lesson has a similar structure and typically follows these classroom routines:

  • The Opening sparks your thinking about the math in the lesson.

  • In Work Time you solve one or more problems on your own and with your partner.

  • Ways of Thinking is a time for you and your classmates to present your mathematical thinking. As a class, you will discuss the different mathematics you and your classmates used to solve the problems.

  • Apply the Learning is a chance for you to work on your own and apply the mathematics you just learned.

  • In Summary of the Math you pull together what you learned in the lesson.

  • Exercises give you an opportunity to work through other problems that build on the mathematics of the lesson.
