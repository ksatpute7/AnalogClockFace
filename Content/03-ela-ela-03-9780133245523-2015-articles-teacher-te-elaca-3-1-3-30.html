Lesson 30

Lesson Overview

Today, students will meet with their Book Clubs. As the teacher, you will be relatively hands-off in the Book Club meetings. You may want to video record snippets of each Book Club and then debrief at the end of the session.

Materials

  • Materials to provide sentence frames (for ELLs)

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Review the Frayer model discussed in Task 1.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 3 and 6.
