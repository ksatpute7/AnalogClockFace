Task 3: Checklist Review

    • Display a piece of your own writing and model editing using the Editing Strategies Checklist. Be sure to Think Aloud so students can hear your thought process.

      Give students a personalized editing checklist to mark off as they work on editing each element of their paper.

    • Begin by rereading the piece. Note a place where it doesn’t make sense because a word is left out, and correct the error.

    • Then read each sentence from beginning to end to make sure it is a complete sentence.

    • Tell students that they should edit for one thing at a time. Point out that when you were reading to make sure it made sense, you read all the way through before moving on to check for complete sentences. Now read through your piece and edit for punctuation only, then read through again editing for spelling only, until you’ve modeled all the editing strategies in the checklist.
