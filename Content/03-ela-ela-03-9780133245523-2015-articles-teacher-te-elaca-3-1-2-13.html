Lesson 13

Lesson Overview

Students discuss the things that make their school a community. They identify and write about the big ideas in their reading. They select a member of their school community to interview, and then brainstorm questions for their interviews.

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and choose differentiation options for working with your students.
  • Look over the annotations for the Common Read What is a Community? from A to Z.
  • Read Interview Preparation on the Teacher Support site so you can give students guidance on conducting interviews.
  • Let staff members of your school know that they may receive emails from you or your students with questions about their roles in the school community.
