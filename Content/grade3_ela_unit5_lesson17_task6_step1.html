Revise Your Cinderella Story

Revise your Cinderella story. Work on making changes to make your story stronger and clearer.

You might choose to do one or more of the following things.

  • Write a more exciting beginning or ending.
  • Delete extra information.
  • Put in details that are missing and that will help the reader understand the culture of the storyteller.
  • Make something that was confusing clearer.
  • Make some of your sentences sound like fairytale language.
