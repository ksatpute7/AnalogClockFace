How to Research

You will need to gather information on an animal that interests you and the problems it could have in a community. Follow along as your teacher models how to conduct research.

Next, conduct your own research! Be sure to write down where you find your information.

  • If you are using one of the websites your teacher recommends, record the following information in your Notebook:
    • Name of the website
    • Address /URL
    • Useful information on this website (in your own words)
  • If you are using an informational text, record the following information in your Notebook:
    • Title of book
    • Author
    • Page number
    • Useful information (in your own words)
