Clues About Culture

As you read today, you will look for clues about culture. You can learn about culture through the characters and setting in a book. The author does not just tell you about the culture; you need to look for clues.

  • Watch as your teacher shows you how to find and use clues to find out about the culture.
  • Turn and Talk with your partner about how you are going to look for clues about culture in your reading today.
