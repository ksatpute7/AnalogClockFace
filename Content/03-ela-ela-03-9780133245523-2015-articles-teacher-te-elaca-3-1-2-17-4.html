Task 4: Spelling Strategies Share

    • Have students share the spelling strategies they used as they checked the spelling in their interview report.

      Emerging P1.3.3

      Have Emerging ELLs Turn and Talk with a partner prior to sharing with the class. Students can share a strategy they used to check their interview report. Provide a sentence frame such as:

      • I used the strategies _____ and _____ to check my spelling in the interview report.

      Expanding P1.3.3

      Have Expanding ELLs Turn and Talk with a partner prior to sharing with the class. Students can share a strategy they used to check their interview report. Expect students to offer their opinions using an expanded sentence frame such as:

      • I used the strategies _____, _____, and _____ to check my spelling in the interview report. These worked well for me because _____.

      Bridging P1.3.3

      Expect Bridging ELLs to offer their opinions using a variety of sentences. Prompt students to elaborate on a strategy by giving a specific example from the interview report.
