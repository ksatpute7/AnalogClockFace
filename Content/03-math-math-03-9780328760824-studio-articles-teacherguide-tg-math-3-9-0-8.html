LESSON 8: The Area of Large Figures

Lesson Overview

Students will find the area of large figures using units other than square centimeters.

Key Concepts

For many real world applications, area is measured in square inches, square feet or square meters, square miles or square kilometers: the amount of carpet needed for a room; the area of a wall in a house; the amount of grass needed to cover a lawn; or the area of a city or state.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Find the area of large figures using appropriate units.

Materials

  • Yardstick (one for each pair of students)

  • Meter sticks (one for each pair of students)

  • Tape measures (one for each pair of students)
