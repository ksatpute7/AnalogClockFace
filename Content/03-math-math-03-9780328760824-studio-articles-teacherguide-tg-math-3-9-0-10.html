LESSON 10: Perimeters of Figures

Lesson Overview

Students will explore the perimeters of rectangles with the same area.

Content Standards

  • 8. Solve real world and mathematical problems involving perimeters of polygons, including finding the perimeter given the side lengths, finding an unknown side length, and exhibiting rectangles with the same perimeter and different areas or with the same area and different perimeters.

Key Concepts

The area of a rectangle is found by multiplying the length by the width. Perimeter is found by doubling the sum of the length and width. Rectangles with the same area may not have the same perimeter.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • To review the formula for perimeter.

  • To understand the difference between area and perimeter.
