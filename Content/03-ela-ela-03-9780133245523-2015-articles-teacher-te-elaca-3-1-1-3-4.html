Task 4: Homework Review

    • Ask students to Turn and Talk with a partner about the book they read for homework. Students should share the title, author, and genre of the text as well as give a brief summary.

    • Having students share their homework with others creates a purpose for completing the work and increases student motivation.

    • In this partner share, students should use the information they just learned about genres when talking about the book they read for homework.
