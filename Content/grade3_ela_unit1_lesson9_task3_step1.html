Word Choice

Authors choose each word carefully to help the reader understand the meaning they intend. If you change a word, you might change the meaning of the sentence.

Listen as your teacher models thinking about how the author’s word choice affects meaning.

Now it is your turn to think about how word choice affects meaning.

  • Find and highlight these words in the text:
    • precious
    • hollered
    • stumbling
    • miracle
  • Work with a partner and do the following for each word:
    • Read closely and decide what the word means.
    • What other words could the author have used?
    • Why do you think the author chose this specific word?
