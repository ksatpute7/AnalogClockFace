LESSON 5: Subtracting Large Numbers

Lesson Overview

Students will solve problems that require them to subtract three-digit numbers.

Key Concepts

Students are familiar with the standard algorithm for subtracting whole numbers from Grade 2. In Grade 2, all the minuends were less than 1,000. In this lesson, the algorithm is extended to include minuends in the thousands. The subtrahends all have three or fewer digits.
