Qualities of Effective Presentations

During Work Time, you often prepare a presentation.

Compare the work Chan and Zoe used in their presentations.

Discuss:

  • How is the work organized?
  • What makes their thinking clear?
  • Why are pictures helpful?

Here are ideas about how to prepare clear presentations:

  • Understand your own work.
  • Show your work in ways that help others know what you are talking about.
  • Say fewer words and choose them carefully.
  • Include pictures and graphs when it is helpful.
  • Speak clearly and slowly.
  • Stay calm. Remember to breathe.
  • Listen carefully to comments and questions.
  • Pause to think about what you want to say before speaking.
