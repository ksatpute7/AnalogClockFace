LESSON 4: Skip-Counting to 1,000

Lesson Overview

Students discuss the important ways that listeners contribute to the mathematical discussions that occur during Ways of Thinking presentations. They make a list of the types of questions they can ask presenters. Students look for and use number patterns to skip-count by 5s and 10s.

Key Concepts

Students look for and use patterns to skip-count by 5s and 10s.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Participate as listeners during the Ways of Thinking discussion

  • Understand the structure of the number sequence in our place value system

  • Skip-count by 5s and 10s

  • Read, write, and say numbers to 1,000

  • Identify patterns in a number chart
