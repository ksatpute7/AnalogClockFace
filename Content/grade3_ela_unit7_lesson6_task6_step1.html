Capitalization in Titles

Watch as your teacher demonstrates which words are capitalized in titles.

Then, participate in a class discussion by suggesting words to capitalize in your teacher’s example titles. Meet with a small group.

  • Capitalize the following two titles in your Notebook:
    • harry potter and the sorcerer’s stone
    • a light in the attic
  • Write a complete sentence using one of the titles.

Share your group’s answers and thinking with the class.
