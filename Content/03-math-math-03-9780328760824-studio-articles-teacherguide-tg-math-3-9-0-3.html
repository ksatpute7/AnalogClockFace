LESSON 3: Square Centimeters

Lesson Overview

Students will be introduced to square centimeters, a unit of measurement for area. Students will find the area of figures and draw figures with a given area.

Key Concepts

The area of a figure can be found and compared to the area of another figure by counting the number of square units. However, even using square units, areas cannot be compared unless each figure is measured using the same square units. So, there is a need for standard area units. Just as centimeters are used to measure length and compare the length of figures, square centimeters are used as an area unit to find and compare the areas of figures measured in those units. A square centimeter is a square with length and width of 1 cm.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Find the area of shapes in square centimeters.

  • Draw shapes with a given area.
