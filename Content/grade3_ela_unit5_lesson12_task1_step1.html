Introduction to Cinderella

All communities tell stories. What if different communities told the same story?  

Discuss the following questions with a partner.

  • Do you think each version of the story would be the same?
  • If not, why do you think that might be?

In this unit, you will read and compare several versions of the Cinderella story. Notice what is the same and what is different in these stories. What can we discover about the communities from which these stories came?

Listen as your teacher reads one version of Cinderella. As you follow along, think about how this story compares to the version of Cinderella you already know.

  • Was it the same?
  • What is different?
