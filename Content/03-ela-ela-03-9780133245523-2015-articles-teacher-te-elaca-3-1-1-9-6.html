Work Time

Task 6: Independent Reading

    • Explain the tasks students will complete during Work Time.

      Shorten and simplify this assignment for your struggling writers by providing them prompts, such as writing about the last book they read, how their reading has improved, and the next book they would like to read.

      Emerging P1.3.3

      Help Emerging ELLs get started with their written response by providing a sentence frame such as:

      • I like to read _____.

      Expanding P1.3.3

      Provide Expanding ELLs with a more complex sentence frame such as:

      • I would like the teacher to know that I _____ as a reader.

      Bridging P1.3.3

      Bridging ELLs should not have difficulty with this task.

    • Have students read independently for 15 minutes, record their reading in their Reading Log, and rate their Independent Reading.

    • Once students have settled into their Work Time tasks, begin your first conference. Limit the time for each conference to about 7 minutes. Take simple notes on what you learned about the student so that you may refer back to these in future conferences.
