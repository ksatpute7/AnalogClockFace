A Mixed-Up Sequence

In Lesson 1, you read Gram’s Angel Food Cake and plotted the events on the Features of Narrative Writing class chart in the order they took place. The chart is covered now.

Sequence is the order of events in a narrative. Add this definition to your Vocabulary form.

Look at the list of events on the sentence strips. They are all mixed up and out of order. Gram’s Angel Food Cake would not make any sense if the events were ordered this way.

Work with your partner to reorder the events in the proper sequence.

  • Reread Gram’s Angel Food Cake.
  • Reorder the events so that they are in the proper sequence. Think about beginning, middle, and end to help you. Use the text to help you.
  • Write down your sequence of events in your Notebook.

Share your new reordered sequence with the class and support your ideas with the text.
