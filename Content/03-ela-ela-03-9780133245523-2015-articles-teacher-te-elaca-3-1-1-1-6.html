Task 6: Community of Readers and Writers

    • One way to form a community in the classroom is to find out as much as we can about one another’s tastes in reading.

    • Lead into this discussion about profiles with an introduction of yourself as a reader and writer. Use the Reader’s and Writer’s Autobiography as a guide.

    • Alternatively, you can use the sample provided.

    • Have students recall the definition of the word autobiography from earlier in this lesson.

    • You may need to explain what is meant by “books in a series” and “comic books.” Have samples to show if possible.
