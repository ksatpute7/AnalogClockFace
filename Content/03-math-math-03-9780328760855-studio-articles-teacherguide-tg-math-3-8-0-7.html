The focus of this lesson is on interpreting and writing fractions greater than one or equal to whole numbers. Next year in Grade 4, students will learn that fractions greater than 1 can be written as mixed numbers, which have a whole number part and a fraction part. For example, 7 4 , which is greater than  1 , can be written as the mixed number 1 3 4 .

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Understand that fractions with the same numerator and denominator are equal to 1.

  • Understand that fractions in which the numerator is greater than the denominator are greater than 1.

  • Write fractions equal to whole numbers.
