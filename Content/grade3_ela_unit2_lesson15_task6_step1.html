Dictionary Definitions

How do you know which definition to choose when you look up a word in the dictionary? Listen and respond as your teacher guides you through the steps to take to choose the right definition.

  • In a paper dictionary, use guide words to help find the word you want.
  • Read all of the definitions for the word you are looking up. Never read just the first one!
  • You will notice that some words can be used as more than one part of speech (adjective, noun, adverb). Figure out the part of speech of the word you are looking for, and find the definition for that part of speech.
  • You should also try to use the definition in your sentence. This can help you to find out which one is correct when there is more than one definition.
