Lesson 4

Lesson Overview

Today, the class will create a rubric for behaviors and habits in Independent Reading.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers
  • Independent Reading chart
  • Materials to provide sentence frames

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Have the Independent Reading chart accessible.
  • Prepare to create an Independent Reading Rubric to share with the class. See the Independent Reading Rubric Sample.
  • Prepare sentence frames for ELLs to use in Tasks 2 and 4.
