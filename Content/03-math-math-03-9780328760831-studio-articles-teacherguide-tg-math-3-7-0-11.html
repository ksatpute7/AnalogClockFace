LESSON 11: Putting It Together

Lesson Overview

Students first review their work on the Self Check problem. Then they work with a partner to write equations, draw arrays or tape diagrams, and write math stories to match a situation. Finally, they revise their work on the Self Check.

Key Concepts

Students demonstrate their understanding of the concept of division. They use what they have learned in this unit to write the multiplication and division equations to match a situation. They then go further by drawing an array or a tape diagram to match the situation. They review the multiplication and division equations that can be written to match the array or tape diagram. Lastly, students write math stories for the situation represented by the diagram.

Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Write equations to match a situation

  • Draw an array or tape diagram to match a situation

  • Write math stories to match a situation

  • Develop understanding of the concept of division

  • Revise the Self Check
