Task 7: Revision Reflection

    • Tell students that one of the most important things they accomplished today was the revision of their interview reports.

    • Identify the constructive comments you heard students giving each other as they shared their reports.

    • Have two to three students share their revision process with the class, reading their work as it was before revision and then reading the revised version.

    • Ask the audience to explain how the revisions made the piece stronger. Compliment the students on their hard work, adding that even published authors go through a revision process!
