Commas in Quotations

You will learn how to correctly place commas into your dialogue.

Quotation marks appear before the first word spoken and after the last word spoken.

If the quotation comes before telling the person who spoke, place a comma after the quote, before the closing quotation mark. Here’s an example.

  • “I am going to the park after school,” said John.

If the quotation comes after telling the person who spoke, place a comma after the person who spoke, before the opening quotation mark.

  • John said, “I am going to the park after school.”
  • Work with a partner. Write down what your partner is going to do after school. Add quotation marks and a comma to the sentence.
