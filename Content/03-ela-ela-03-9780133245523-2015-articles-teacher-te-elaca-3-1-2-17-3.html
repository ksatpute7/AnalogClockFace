Task 3: Interview Report Spell Check

    • Give students time to check their spelling on their interview report. Confer with them as they do this. Encourage students to be aware of the spelling strategies they have found to be most helpful so that they can share these with the class during Closing.

    • Remind students to proofread their writing.

      SWDs may be reluctant to contribute ideas during the Whole Group Discussion for a variety of reasons. Circulate as students write so that you can coach students and suggest which of their ideas may be interesting to share.

      Emerging P1.3.10

      Bring Emerging ELLs together in a small group and jointly revise their spelling. Provide students with a Spelling Strategies Checklist and review the strategies with students. Have bilingual dictionaries available, and direct them to the classroom Word Walls to use as reference. Prior to joining the group have students practice describing their favorite strategy so they feel prepared for the Whole Group Discussion.

      Expanding P1.3.10

      Allow Expanding ELLs to work independently or with a partner. Provide them with a Spelling Strategies Checklist. Have bilingual dictionaries available and direct them to the classroom Word Walls to use as a reference.

      Bridging P1.3.10

      Have Bridging ELLs work independently. Provide them with a Spelling Strategies Checklist and a bilingual dictionary to reference.
