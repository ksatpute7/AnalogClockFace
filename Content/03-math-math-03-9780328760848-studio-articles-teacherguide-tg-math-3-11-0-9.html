LESSON 9: Gallery

Gallery Overview

Allow students who have a clear understanding of the content thus far in the unit to work on Gallery problems of their choosing. You can then use this time to provide additional help to students who need review of the unit's concepts or to assist students who may have fallen behind on work.

Gallery Descriptions

Grams and Kilograms

Students will find objects that weigh close to 1 kg, 10 g, and between 100 g and 200 g. Next, they solve problems using the weights of the objects.

Tall Skinny, Short Stout

Students estimate the volumes of several liquid containers. Then students use a liter cube to measure the volumes and compare estimates with actual measurements.

Balances and Spring Scales

Students explore different ways to measure weight. They use a bathroom scale, a spring scale, and a balance to solve problems.

A Design Challenge

Students are challenged to create a work of art weighing exactly 40 g. They use four different materials that each weigh 10 g.
