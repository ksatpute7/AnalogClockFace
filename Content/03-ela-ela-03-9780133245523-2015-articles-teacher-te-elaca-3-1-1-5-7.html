Task 7: Vocabulary Review

    • Take a few minutes to review the academic vocabulary introduced in the course of this lesson.

    • Ask students:

      Can I write your autobiography? (no)

      What is an expectation your family has of you? (to help with chores)

      Do you enjoy a good narrative? (Yes, I like a good story.)

      What is one genre you like? (mysteries)

      How many people are needed to have a discussion? (at least two)

      Would a teacher use a rubric to grade a piece of writing? (yes)

    • Ask students if they have questions about any of the six words and clarify as necessary.
