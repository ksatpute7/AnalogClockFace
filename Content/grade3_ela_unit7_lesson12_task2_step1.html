Partner Share

Discuss the following questions with a partner. Be sure to use your three-column chart to help with your discussion.

  • What problems do these animals have in their communities?
  • What patterns do you see?

While you are talking with your partner, you may discover a possible solution to one of the problems you noticed with an animal. If this happens, write down your solution in the third column on your chart.

Write down what you and your partner are thinking. Then, share your thinking with the rest of the class.
