Task 4: What Is a Community?

    • Read the A, B, and C pages of What is a Community? from A to Z, modeling how you identify the important ideas in the text (see the model annotation).

      Students may have difficulty identifying important ideas in text. Provide a list of Guiding Questions for them to answer as they read as a means of helping them recognize salient points. Review the questions with them before they begin reading so they will be better able to identify the information they are seeking.

    • Help students understand that the word community has two different meanings, one related to groups and one related to places. Discuss both and identify them in relation to your students.
