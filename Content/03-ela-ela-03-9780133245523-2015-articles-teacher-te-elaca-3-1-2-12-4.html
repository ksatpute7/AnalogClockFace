Task 4: Writing: What Is a Community?

    • Monitor as students do their Quick Writes and partner shares. Identify student ideas that you might want students to share with the whole class in the Closing.

      Emerging P1.3.2

      Emerging ELLs can benefit from collaborating with peers on a joint writing of the Quick Write. Encourage students to include basic sentences to support their opinions, such as:

      • I think _____.

      Expanding P1.3.2

      Expanding ELLs can benefit from collaborating with peers on a joint writing of the Quick Write. Encourage students to include expanded sentences to offer opinions. Provide sentence frames such as:

      • I believe the main idea is _____.

      Bridging P1.3.2

      Bridging ELLs can complete the Quick Write at the same level as their English-proficient peers.
