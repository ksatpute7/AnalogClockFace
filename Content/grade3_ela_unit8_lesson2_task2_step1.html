Paint a Picture in Your Head

A stanza in a poem is like a paragraph in a story.

  • Circle each of the stanzas in “The Land of Counterpane.”

Listen to your teacher read aloud the first part (stanza) of the poem “The Land of Counterpane” by Robert Louis Stevenson. Think about the pictures the poem creates in your mind.

  • Do Robert Louis Stevenson’s words help you to visualize what is happening in the poem?
  • Which words help you visualize what is happening the most?
