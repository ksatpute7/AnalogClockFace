Lesson 14

Lesson Overview

Students learn the parts of an email and construct one to send to their selected community member. They begin to draft their report, and exchange feedback with a partner on each other’s writing.

Materials

  • Email checklist and model email message

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Review Introductory Interview Email on the Teacher Support site prior to the lesson.
  • In this lesson, students will craft a message to a community member. They will submit their notes to you so that you can email them to the community members.
  • Inform the people in your school community—those who were chosen by your students to interview—that they will receive an email and ask for their support by providing a quick response.
  • Create a checklist of all components of an email (i.e., heading, message body, and signature) and write a model email message.
