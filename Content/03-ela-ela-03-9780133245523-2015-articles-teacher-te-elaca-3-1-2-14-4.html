Task 4: Email to Your Community Member

    • Monitor as students write their messages. Provide support where needed. If you notice a group of students having difficulty with this task, pull them aside for additional instruction.

      Emerging P1.3.10a

      Prompt and support Emerging ELLs during the process of writing the email. Pose basic questions that will allow students to generate the simple sentences for the email. Have students state the sentences they will write, and have a more English-proficient student write down the sentences for the group. Give students an Email checklist of all the components of the email to use as a guide. In addition, have a model email message available as a reference for the group.

      Expanding P1.3.10a

      Provide Expanding ELL partnerships with an Email checklist of all the components and a model email message as a reference. As you monitor students, check in on the partnerships and provide occasional prompting and support as they draft their emails.

      Bridging P1.3.10a

      Provide Bridging ELL partnerships with an Email checklist of the components of the email as a reference. As you monitor students, provide minimal prompting and light support as they construct their responses.
