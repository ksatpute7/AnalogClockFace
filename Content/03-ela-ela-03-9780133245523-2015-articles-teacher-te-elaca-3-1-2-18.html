Lesson 18

Lesson Overview

Students will learn about editing and will share their editing experiences.

Materials

  • Chart paper and markers for the Editing Strategies Checklist

Lesson Preparation

  • Read the lesson and student content.
  • Anticipate student difficulties and identify the differentiation options you will choose for working with your students.
  • Prepare a writing sample of your own to use when modeling the Editing Strategies Checklist.
