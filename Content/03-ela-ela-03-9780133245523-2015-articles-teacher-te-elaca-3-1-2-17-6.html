      Emerging P1.3.1

      During the Independent Reading time, preview the Book Talk activity with Emerging ELLs. Provide ELLs with a Book Talk graphic organizer. Review each part and model how to complete it. Allow ELLs to complete the graphic organizer during the Independent Reading block in anticipation of the discussion. Explain to ELLs that all students will participate in Book Talks and they will be able to use their notes and this graphic organizer when sharing. Expect students to use their graphic organizer to share using short sentences or simple phrases.

      Expanding P1.3.1

      During the Independent Reading time, preview the Book Talk activity with Expanding ELLs. Provide students with a Book Talk checklist. Encourage students to jot down notes to reference during the discussion. Expect students to share using expanded sentences.

      Bridging P1.3.1

      Provide Bridging ELLs with a Book Talk Checklist. Remind students that after the Independent Reading time, they will participate in Book Talks. Expect students to share using a variety of complex sentences.
