Task 2: What Numbers Are Missing?

Lesson Guide

Remind students that they worked on number charts in the last lesson.

To fill in the missing numbers in the chart, have students follow the ritual you have been using in previous lessons.

  • Think about the question on their own

  • Turn and Talk about their ideas with their partner

  • Discuss their ideas by sharing them with the class

Have students discuss the missing numbers as a class until they come to agreement. Then ask students the following question.

  • Your chart of the 200s numbers only goes to 249. Can you skip-count from 250 to 300 by 10s without looking at the chart?

Lead the group in the skip-count. If students need help, write each number as students say it to provide visual support: 250, 260, 270, 280, 290, 300.

Then have students skip-count together by 5s from 250 to 300 without looking at the chart.

End the discussion by showing students how to access the Hundred Chart tool in their Notebook; explain that they use the chart to work on the next task.
