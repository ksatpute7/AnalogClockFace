Phonics: Inflected Endings/Suffixes
  • You add -s or -es to a root word to show that a word is plural. Plural means more than one.
  • Plurals like -s and -es are called inflectional endings because they tell us more about the word.
  • Adding the ending -es can also change the number of syllables. With your teacher, practice adding the ending -es to words.
