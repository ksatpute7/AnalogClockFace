Task 5: Prepare a Presentation

Lesson Guide

Have students work individually to prepare a presentation.

Preparing for Ways of Thinking

Look for students who used different patterns in the number chart to explain both correct and incorrect responses. Have these students discuss their approaches during Ways of Thinking.
