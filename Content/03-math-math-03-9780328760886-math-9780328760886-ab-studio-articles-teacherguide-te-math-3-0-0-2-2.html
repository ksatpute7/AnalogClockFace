Task 2: Classroom Expectations

Lesson Guide

Tell students that in order to make each math class run smoothly, they need to understand and follow certain classroom expectations.

Refer students to the Classroom Expectations list or the classroom poster, if you made one.

Discuss the following classroom expectations.

  • The procedure for gathering materials, including the tablet

  • The procedure and locations for putting away materials at the end of the lesson

  • The ritual for submitting exercises (when and how)

  • Any other rituals that fit with the classroom norms and will help the class run more smoothly

Share this list of classroom expectations with the students.

Consider taking students on a tour of the classroom to show them where they will gather materials, other designated spaces in the classroom, and areas such as the Word Wall.

ELL:

When working with ELLs, it is important to be as explicit as possible in establishing rituals. You can use gestures, pointing, and other cues as you give students the tour of the classroom.
