Work Time
Annotation: Turtles & Tortoises

Annotate Turtles & Tortoises with your responses to your teacher’s instructions.

Open to the second chapter, “Turtle or Tortoise?” and do the following tasks as instructed by your teacher.

  • Find the paragraph that tells about two types of turtles. Use annotation to note in your own words what the paragraph says.
  • Highlight the sentence that tells how a tortoise is different than a turtle.
  • Read the next paragraph and use annotation to describe physical differences between turtles and tortoises. Share what you find with the class.
  • Turn to the chapter “Life of a Turtle: From Egg to Adult.” Underline and number the steps that a turtle goes through to reach adulthood. Share your responses with the class.
