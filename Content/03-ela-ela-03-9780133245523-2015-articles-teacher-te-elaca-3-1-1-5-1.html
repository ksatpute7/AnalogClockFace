Opening

Task 1: Homework Share

    • Ask students to Turn and Talk with a partner about what they read for homework.

      Emerging P1.3.3

      Have Emerging ELLs offer opinions with a partner using basic sentence frames to refer to their reading. For example:

      • Last night I read _____.
      • The author is _____.
      • The genre is _____.
      • I liked/did not like it because _____.

      Expanding P1.3.3

      Have Expanding ELLs offer opinions with a partner using an expanded set of sentence frames to refer to their reading. These students can also use open responses to help sustain conversation and provide responses. For example:

      • I read _____. It was a good/not good choice because _____.
      • I agree with your choice of book because _____.

      Bridging P1.3.3

      Have Bridging ELLs offer opinions with a partner using a variety of sentence frames to refer to their reading. These students can elaborate on their ideas. For example:

      • _____ is a book by _____. It was an interesting book to read because _____.
      • I recommend this book because ______.
      • You should read _____ because _____.
