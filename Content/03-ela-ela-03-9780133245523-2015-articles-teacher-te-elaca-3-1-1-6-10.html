Work Time

Task 10: Rephrasing: Independent Reading

    • Have students write a response in their Notebook that rephrases the text they read during Independent Reading.

    • Explain that their rephrasing should include all the important events and explain what happened in the book. Provide examples and sentence starters if necessary.

      Some students may need to meet with an adult prior to writing to help them clarify their ideas. Others may be able to plan with a partner but may need help in getting their ideas down on paper, so they may need someone (partner, adult) to do the writing. Encourage struggling writers to sketch pictures while they wait to meet with you or another scribe. Students should leave Work Time with one set of actions on their planning sheet so they have a clear plan for writing.
