Day 2: Closed Sort

  • Review the words with the students. In the review, focus on pattern and word meaning.

    • agree
    • braid
    • cheese
    • clean
    • coach
    • display
    • dream
    • float
    • grain
    • peach
    • shadow
    • Sunday
    • teeth
    • thrown
    • window

  • Remind students that on the previous day, they looked at words to see what makes them similar and what makes them different.

  • Students are probably familiar enough to do the Closed Sort without instruction. If necessary, remind them how it works by explaining why agree/clean, braid/display, and coach/shadow all go into separate categories due to the ee/ea, ai/ay, and oa/ow sorting criteria. Make note of each spelling pattern and the sound as you do so.

  • Have partners complete the sort with the words.

  • Review the results of the sort. Ask students to explain why they made the specific choices, or have them make a generalization about the words in each category.

  • Share the spelling pattern for each sound with the class. Have students add other words that follow these patterns in their Spelling Notebook.
