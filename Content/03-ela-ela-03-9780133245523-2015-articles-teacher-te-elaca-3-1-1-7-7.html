Task 7: Sentence Deconstruction

    • Work with students to deconstruct a complex sentence. This is an important skill for all students, but especially for those who are unfamiliar with academic language.

    • Read the sentence aloud.

    • Then ask:

      • What task were Katje and her mama busy with?
      • What three things were involved in this task?
      • What was being rubbed off the stems?
      • What are bulbs and why would they sort them?
      • Note the word while. This word indicates that two things were happening at the same time. What were the things?
