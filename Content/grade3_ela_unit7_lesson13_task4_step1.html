Poster Work

It’s now time for you to decide on the text structures you will use in your poster project. Which text structures will give the reader the clearest understanding of your topic?

You may choose to use more than one text structure, as the author did in Turtles & Tortoises. Look at the informational texts you used to collect information on your animal—which text structures did these authors use? You may consider using similar text structures in your writing.

  • Begin to draft sections of your poster based on the information you have collected and organized.
  • After you have completed a draft of your poster, send a copy to your teacher for feedback. Once you have received feedback, you may start creating your poster. Let your teacher know if you have any problems.

Remember, your project should be completed by Lesson 16.
